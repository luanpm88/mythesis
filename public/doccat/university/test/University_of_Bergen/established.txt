<p>In 2010 the university was ranked as number 135 worldwide by the <a>Times Higher Education World University Rankings</a>, and as number 133 worldwide by the <a>QS World University Rankings</a>.
UiB was also ranked number 148 worldwide in the July 2010 <a>Webometrics Ranking of World Universities</a>.</p>
<p>The faculty revised its structure and names in August 2007.</p>
<p>The Faculty of Law was established as a separate faculty in 1980, with legal studies and research having been conducted at the university since 1969.
<li>Dental University Hospital</li>
<p>The University of Bergen is the only institution in the Nordic countries where the study of <a>psychology</a> has been assigned to its own faculty.
