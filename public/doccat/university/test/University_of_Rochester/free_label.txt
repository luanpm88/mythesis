<p>The song most often sung at college events, led often by the school's many <a>a cappella</a> groups, is <em><a>The Genesee</a></em>.
<p><strong>Eastman School of Music Campus</strong> Housing is provided at the <a>Eastman School of Music</a> campus at the <strong>Eastman Student Living Center</strong> at 100 Gibbs Street in downtown Rochester.
<p><strong>South Campus</strong> The South Campus has graduate student housing at the <strong>Whipple Park</strong> (<strong>WPK</strong>) complex, which features 250 garden apartments and townhouses with ample storage space.
