<p>University of Rochester's <a>undergraduate</a> enrollment consists of about 4,500 full-time and about 100 part-time students from across the U.S. and over 90 countries.
<p><strong>River Campus</strong> River Campus residences house primarily undergraduates, with some graduate students serving as Graduate Head Residents (GHRs).
