<p>The university rose to pronounced significance during the rise of <a>Sweden as a Great Power</a> at the end of the 16th century and was then given a relative financial stability with the large donation of King <a>Gustavus Adolphus</a> in the early 17th century.
The university has nine faculties distributed over three 'disciplinary domains'.
The aspirations of the emergent new great power of Sweden demanded a different kind of learning.
