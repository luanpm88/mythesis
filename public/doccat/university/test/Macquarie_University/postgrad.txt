<p>The university comprises four faculties, enrolling approximately 35,000 students and having 2,221 (full-time equivalent) academic and professional staff, making it the fourth largest University in Sydney.
<p>The university first opened to students on 6 March 1967 with more students than anticipated.
Upon completion of the GLP, students receive a formal notation on their academic transcript.</p>
Upon successful completion of a SIBT Diploma, students enter the appropriate Bachelor Degree as a second year student.</p>
