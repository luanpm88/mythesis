<p>Students at Occidental can take courses at the <a>California Institute of Technology</a> in nearby <a>Pasadena</a> free of charge.
In addition, a 3-2 engineering program allows qualified students the opportunity to study at Occidental for three years, completing their undergraduate experience with an additional two years either at <a>Caltech</a> or <a>Columbia University</a>.
The <em>College Prowler</em> says that people &#x201c;look at Occidental degrees very highly,&#x201d; but that Occidental often does not receive the attention it deserves.</p>
