<p>In February 2007, the university agreed to acquire a portion of the land granted to <a>St John's College</a> to develop the Sydney Institute of Health and Medical Research.
It retains the same University Arms, however takes on a more modern look.
The Coat of Arms continues to be used for more formal purposes, such as on testamurs.</p>
Although the organisations continue to be concerned about their long-term financial viability, they have secured significant funding from the university to partially make up for lost revenue.</p>
