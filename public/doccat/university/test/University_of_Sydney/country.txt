<p>Originally housed in what is now <a>Sydney Grammar School</a>, in 1855, the government granted the university land in <a>Grose Farm</a>, three kilometres from the city, which is now the main <a>Camperdown</a> campus.
<li>The Sydney Dental Hospital located in <strong>Surry Hills</strong> and the <strong>Westmead Centre for Oral Health</strong> which is attached to Westmead Hospital.
<li>The <strong><a>Macleay Museum</a></strong> is named after Alexander Macleay, whose collection of insects begun in the late eighteenth century was the basis upon which the museum was founded.
Elections for the USU Board of Directors occur in first semester; elections for the SRC President, and for members of the Students' Representative Council itself, occur in second semester, along with a separate election for the editorial board of the student newspaper <em><a>Honi Soit</a></em>, which is published by the SRC.
