<p> The <strong>University of Manitoba</strong>, in <a>Winnipeg</a>, <a>Manitoba</a>, <a>Canada</a>, is the largest university in the province of <a>Manitoba</a>.
<p>The policy of university education initiated in the 1960s responded to population pressure.&lt;ref
The remaining buildings contain laboratories, administrative and service offices, residences, or are the property of research agencies.</p>
<p>The professors are represented by two different unions.
The professors are represented by the <a>University of Manitoba Faculty Association</a>,&lt;ref&gt;<a>http://www.umanitoba.ca/admin/human_resources/staff_relations/academic/1268.htm</a>&lt;/ref&gt; while the part-time professors and teaching assistants are represented by the <a>CUPE</a> Local 3909.&lt;ref&gt;<a>http://www.umanitoba.ca/admin/human_resources/staff_relations/academic/1265.htm</a>&lt;/ref&gt;&lt;ref&gt;<a>http://www.umanitoba.ca/admin/human_resources/staff_relations/academic/1266.htm</a>&lt;/ref&gt;
The support staff and the campus security are represented by the <a>AESES</a>,&lt;ref&gt;<a>http://www.umanitoba.ca/admin/human_resources/staff_relations/support/1271.htm</a>&lt;/ref&gt;
All of the outside workers are represented by the <a>CAW</a> Local 3007.&lt;ref&gt;<a>http://www.umanitoba.ca/admin/human_resources/staff_relations/support/1273.htm</a>&lt;/ref&gt;</p>
<p>The <a>National Panhellenic Council</a> sororities on campus are <a>Alpha Delta Pi</a>, <a>Alpha Gamma Delta</a>, and <a>Alpha Phi</a>.&lt;ref&gt;&lt;/ref&gt;
