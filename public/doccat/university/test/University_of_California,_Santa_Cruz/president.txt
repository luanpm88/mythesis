<h3>Impact on Santa Cruz</h3>
The oldest fraternity on campus is Theta Chi, which was given colony status on January 10, 1987 and later became a full chapter on October 14, 1989 (designation: Theta Iota).
Sororities on campus include Gamma Phi Beta, Kappa Kappa Gamma, Tri-Chi, Kappa Zeta, and Alpha Psi.
