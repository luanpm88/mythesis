In July 1988, the <a>Sisters of Charity of Saint Vincent de Paul</a> officially transferred ownership of the institution to the Board of Governors.</p>
<p>The Department of Applied Human Nutrition has an accredited dietetic program.
The University is accredited by a professional organization such as the <a>Dietitians of Canada</a> and the university's graduates may subsequently become registered dietitians.
<p>Academic programs are supported by a wide variety of electronic and print research resources in the MSVU Library.
The Mystics hold a number of regional championship titles in all sports.
