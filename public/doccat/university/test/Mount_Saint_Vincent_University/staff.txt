<p><strong>Mount Saint Vincent University</strong> is a <a>university</a> located in <a>Halifax</a>, <a>Nova Scotia</a>, <a>Canada</a>.
<p><a>left|200px|thumb|Mount Saint Vincent University Entrance from the Bedford Highway, Halifax</a></p>
<p>Established by the <a>Sisters of Charity of Saint Vincent de Paul</a> in 1873, the Mount was one of the only institutions of higher education for women in Canada at a time when women could not vote.
<p>By 1912, the <a>Sisters of Charity of Saint Vincent de Paul</a> recognized the need to offer greater opportunity through university education and adopted a progressive plan to establish a college for young women.

<p>During the <a>1995 G7 summit</a>, Mount Saint Vincent University awarded an honorary Doctor of Laws degree to <a>Hillary Rodham Clinton</a>.</p>
