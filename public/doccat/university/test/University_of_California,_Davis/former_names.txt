<p>The central campus is bounded by freeways on two sides (Highway 113 and <a>Interstate 80</a>).
One, off Highway 113, is signed &quot;UC Davis / Hutchison Drive&quot; and the other, off Interstate 80, is signed exclusively as &quot;UC Davis.&quot;</p>
<p>Admission to UC Davis is rated as &quot;most selective&quot; by <a>U.S. News and World Report</a>.&lt;ref&gt;&lt;/ref&gt;</p>
<p>The Panehellenic Council is similar to the Interfraternity Council, but is the governing council for many sororities at UC Davis.
AGR Hall is located inside the Buehler Alumni / Visitor's Center and is commonly used as a conference room.
<p>The Aggie football team plays <a>Sacramento State</a> in the annual <a>Causeway Classic</a> for the <a>Causeway Carriage</a>.
In 2007, UC Davis wrestler Derek Moore gained All-American status, as well as won the NCAA Division I Championships for his weight class.
<p>The official school mascot is the <a>mustang</a>.
<p>UC Davis is also home to the <a>Agricultural Sustainability Institute (ASI)</a>, which is part of the College of Agricultural and Environmental Sciences (CAES).
