<p>Other student activities</p>
<li><a>Unitrans</a>, the student run (and driven) bus system.</li>
<li>The <a>Coffee House</a>, also known as the CoHo, is a student run restaurant serving 7000 customers daily.</li>
<li><a>KDVS</a>, student radio.&lt;ref&gt;<a>http://kdvs.org/</a>&lt;/ref&gt;</li>
<p>Initially, the <em>Weekly Agricola</em> was focused on both student news and farming-related topics.
Many campus buildings are named after alumni of Alpha Gamma Rho such as Emil Mrak (Mrak Hall, Registrar's office), Orville Thompson (Thompson Hall, Segundo student housing), and Dean De Carli (the De Carli room, 2nd floor MU), Mel Olson Scoreboard (Aggie Stadium), and many more.
