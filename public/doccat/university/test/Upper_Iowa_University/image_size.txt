<p>UIU offers <a>distance education</a> programs that include 15 centers in the U.S., an <a>online program</a>, an <a>independent study</a> program, and centers in <a>Hong Kong</a>, <a>Singapore</a>, and <a>Malaysia</a>.
<p>In 1972, Upper Iowa launched an external degree program that included an Independent Study and Online Programs.
<p>It was also during 1994 to 2003 that UIU underwent an aggressive landscaping and building renovation that brought changes to its Fayette Campus.
