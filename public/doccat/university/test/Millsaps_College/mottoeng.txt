The writing-intensive core curriculum requires each student to compile an acceptable portfolio of written work before completion of the sophomore year.
The student to faculty ratio is 11:1 with an average class size around 15 students.
The Millsaps campus is close to downtown Jackson.
It is bordered by Woodrow Wilson Avenue to the north, North State Street to the east, West Street to the west, and Marshall Street to the south.</p>
The Nicholson Garden was added to improve the aesthetics of this area.
