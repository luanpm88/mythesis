With opportunities such as <a>co-operative education</a> and <a>internships</a>, <a>teaching practicums</a> and <a>nursing placements</a>, <a>international exchange agreements</a>, faculty exchanges, and students doing real-world research alongside their professors &#x2014; on campus and off &#x2014; students are applying what they learn in the classroom to the world around them.</p>
