The teams are members of the <a>Old Dominion Athletic Conference</a> (ODAC), which has 14 member institutions.</p>
The team won the ODAC championship in 2007 as well under highly respected coach, Sarah Irvine who won the Coach of the Year title.
In 2001, the team made the school's first ever national championship game by playing in the NCAA Division III national championship, the Amos Alonzo Stagg Bowl.</p>
<p>There are also a few longstanding traditions held independent of the college by members of the student body.
