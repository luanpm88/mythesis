<p> <strong>Bridgewater College</strong>, is a <a>private</a>, <a>coeducational</a>, four-year <a>liberal-arts</a> <a>college</a> historically affiliated with the <a>Church of the Brethren</a>.
The college is located in <a>Bridgewater, Virginia</a>, a town in <a>Rockingham County</a>, <a>Virginia</a>, <a>United States</a>.
