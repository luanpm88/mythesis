<p>As of Spring 2010, UH Hilo offers 36 undergraduate, 6 graduate, and 2 doctoral degree programs.
Trade winds bring abundant rains, occasionally heavy, with most of the precipitation tending to fall at night.
Due to regular rainfall, there are numerous covered walkways located between most of the buildings throughout UH Hilo's main campus.</p>
Hawaiian is the medium of instruction and communication among students, staff, and administration at the laboratory schools, which focus on college preparation, environmental and health studies, sustainable agriculture, and teacher training.
The men's and women's soccer and women's basketball athletic programs were added in 2006.</p>
