Cal Poly Pomona has transitioned from an agricultural <a>two-year technical college</a> with an inaugural enrollment of 110 all-men students into a <a>master&#x2019;s university</a> currently offering multiple educational programs in <a>9 academic units</a> while having a <a>coeducational</a> enrollment of over 20,000 students as of fall 2009.
The university has raised admissions standards, increased on-campus student residences, built new facilities and expanded <a>its undergraduate research opportunities</a>.
Contrasting some of this architecturally prominent facilities, there are various <a>portable building</a>s on campus which are used to accommodate the growing population of recent decades.
