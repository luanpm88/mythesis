<p> <strong>Washington &amp; Jefferson College</strong>, also known as <strong>W &amp; J College</strong> or <strong>W&amp;J</strong>, is a private <a>liberal arts college</a> in <a>Washington, Pennsylvania</a>, which is  south of <a>Pittsburgh</a>.
<p>The college's academic emphasis is on the liberal arts and the sciences, with a focus on preparing students for graduate and professional schools.
Across all disciplines, 85% to 90% of students who apply to such programs receive offers of admission.
Among students who apply to medical school or related health graduate programs, 90% of students gain admission.
<p>In the 2010 <em><a>U.S. News &amp; World Report</a></em> college rankings, Washington &amp; Jefferson is ranked #92 of all liberal arts colleges in the nation, placing it within &quot;Tier 1.&quot;  <em><a>The Washington Monthly</a></em> ranked the college #132 among all American liberal arts colleges in terms of <a>social mobility</a>, research, and <a>community service</a>.
