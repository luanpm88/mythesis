The college maintains a number of combined degree programs, allowing students to attend <a>graduate</a> or professional school in lieu of senior year.
<p> As of 2010, the student body totaled 1,519 students.
The college's <a>yearbook</a>, <em>Pandora</em>, is produced annually by a student staff.</p>
As of 2009, Washington &amp; Jefferson College had about 12,000 living <a>alumni</a>.
<a>William Holmes McGuffey</a> authored the <em><a>McGuffey Readers</a></em>, which are among the most popular and influential books in history.
