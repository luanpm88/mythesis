<p>The historical phrases <em>Quod natura non dat, Salamantica non praestat</em> (what nature does not give, Salamanca does not lend, in latin) and <em>Multos et doctissimos Salmantica habet</em> (many and very versed Salamanca has) give an idea of the prestige the institution rapidly acquired.</p>
<p>In 2009, preparations were being made for the celebration of the institution's eighth centennial.</p>
