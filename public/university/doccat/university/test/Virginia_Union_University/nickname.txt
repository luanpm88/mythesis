VUU's  campus is located at 1500 North Lombardy Street in <a>Richmond's North Side</a>.</p>
The Belgian government sponsored a competition to determine the building's new home.
<p>Virginia Union competes in the <a>NCAA</a> <a>Division II</a> in the Eastern Division of the <a>Central Intercollegiate Athletic Association</a>.
The school has varsity teams in men's basketball, football, cross country, golf, tennis and track and field, and in women's basketball, bowling, cross country, tennis and track and field, softball and volleyball.</p>
