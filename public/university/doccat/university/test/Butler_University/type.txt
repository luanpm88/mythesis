<p>  <strong>Butler University</strong> is a private, co-educational <a>liberal arts</a> <a>university</a> in <a>Indianapolis</a>, <a>Indiana</a>, <a>United States</a>.
The University also provides student research opportunities, such as the Butler Summer Institute, a 10-week program where Butler students are granted funding to perform independent research with a faculty member.</p>
