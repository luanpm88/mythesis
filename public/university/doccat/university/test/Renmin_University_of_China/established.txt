<p> <strong>Renmin University of China</strong>; <strong>RUC</strong>, previously known as <strong>People's University of China</strong> (), colloquially <strong><a>Renda</a></strong> (), is  a national comprehensive university located in <a>Beijing</a>, <a>China</a>.
<p>Renmin University is commonly considered as one of the top universities in China, with a distinct focus on humanities and social sciences.
Renmin University is particularly known for its strength in law, economics, and journalism.</p>
<h2>History</h2>
<p><a>thumb|230px|left|The rock holding the engraved motto of the university</a> The predecessor of the university was <strong>Shan Bei Public School</strong> (&#x9655;&#x5317;&#x516c;&#x5b66;), established in 1937 during the <a>Second Sino-Japanese War</a>.
Later it was renamed the <strong>North China United University</strong> and <strong>North China University</strong>. Renmin University of China was officially established in 1950, the first national university of <a>People's Republic of China</a>.
<a>Wu Yuzhang</a>, <a>Cheng Fangwu</a>, <a>Guo Yingqiu</a>, <a>Yuan Baohua</a>, <a>Huang Da</a>, and <a>Li Wenhai</a> have successively held the presidential posts.
The current university president is Prof. <a>Ji Baocheng</a>, a renowned expert in trade economics and educational administration.</p>
The University library has 2.5 million holdings, and is recognized as the <a>Information Center of Arts Literatures</a> by the <a>Ministry of Education</a>.
The new library building is under construction at this time.
<p>Renmin University has established its communications with 125 universities and research institutions of 32 countries and regions, which enables the University to be the center of academic and cultural communications between China and foreign countries.
Many famous scholars have given lectures or attended special seminars in Renmin University, including the <a>Nobel Prize in Economics</a> winners <a>Robert Mundell</a>, <a>Joseph E. Stiglitz</a>, <a>Michael Spence</a>, <a>John Forbes Nash</a>, <a>Edmund S. Phelps</a> and <a>Reinhard Selten</a>.
Generally speaking Renmin University is considered to be the best place for students to study social sciences in China.
Renmin University is located at No.59 <a>Zhongguancun</a> Street, <a>Haidian</a> district, and is less than 5&#x2013;10 minutes drive away from other top schools in the nation, for example, <a>Peking University</a>, <a>Tsinghua University</a>, <a>Beijing Institute of Technology</a> and <a>Beijing Foreign Studies University</a>.
It is also only less than 10 minutes away from the <a>Old Summer Palace</a> and <a>Summer Palace</a>.</p>
<p>The <a>English Corner</a> at Renmin University is very famous in <a>Beijing</a>.
Every Friday evening, people gather at the Qiushi Garden near the east gate to practice English.
It is a good place to meet and communicate with the students of the university and the common people of China.</p>
