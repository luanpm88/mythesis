<p><strong>Herkimer County Community College</strong> is a two-year <a>community college</a> in the <a>Village of Herkimer</a> in <a>Herkimer County, New York</a>, <a>USA</a>.
The college is in the northwest part of the village, bounded on the north by Reservoir Road.
<h2>History </h2>
<p>The initial planning for Herkimer Community College began with a meeting in 1962 in <a>Dolgeville, New York</a>.
The college was approved by the <a>State University of New York</a> in 1965, and the County Board of Supervisors authorized the college in 1966.
The first class, numbering 221 freshmen, entered in the Fall of 1967 at a temporary campus in the <a>Village of Ilion</a>.
The temporary campus was the upper floors of the <a>Remington Arms Company</a>.
The current, permanent campus came into use in 1971.
<p>The college offers programs leading to <a>associate's degree</a>s or certificates in several dozen program areas.
The college is fully accredited by the <a>Middle States Association of Colleges and Schools</a>.
<p>Herkimer County Community College's Generals have won a total of 32 <a>NJCAA</a> championships in women's <a>field hockey</a>, men's <a>nordic</a>, men's <a>lacrosse</a>, women's <a>lacrosse</a>, men's <a>basketball</a>, men's <a>soccer</a>, women's <a>soccer</a>, <a>cross country running</a>, and the <a>track &amp; field</a> events of women's <a>pole vault</a> and women's <a>100 meter dash</a>.</p>
