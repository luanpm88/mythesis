<p> <strong>Valencia Community College</strong> is a multi-campus <a>college</a> located in <a>Orlando, Florida</a> in the <a>United States</a>, and is third-largest institution of the <a>Florida College System</a>.</p>
<li><strong>Downtown Center</strong> Valencia's Downtown Center is located in the historic Chicone Building in downtown Orlando, at the corner of Orange Avenue and Church Street.
Valencia's Center for Global Languages prepares students for entry into a college degree program.</li>
