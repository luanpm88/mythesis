<p>The main MIPT campus is located in <a>Dolgoprudny</a>, a northern suburb of <a>Moscow</a>.
However the <a>Aeromechanics Faculty</a> is based in <a>Zhukovsky</a>, a suburb south-east of Moscow.</p>
<li>Conducting their education in an atmosphere of research and creative engineering, using the best existing laboratories in the country.</li>
Classes are taught five days a week, beginning at 9:00 am or 10:30 am, and continuing until 5:00 pm, 6:30 pm, or 8:00 pm.
Most subjects include a combination of lectures and seminars (problem-solving study sessions in smaller groups) or laboratory experiments.
Most importantly, starting with the third year, students begin work at <em>base institutes</em> (or &quot;base organizations,&quot; usually simply called <em>bases</em>).
