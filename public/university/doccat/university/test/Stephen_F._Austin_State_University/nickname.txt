In tribute to the forest industry, which is a major component of the area's economy, the men's athletic teams are called <a>Lumberjacks</a>, and women's teams are known as Ladyjacks.
<p>Stephen F. Austin sports teams participate in NCAA Division I (<a>Football Championship Subdivision</a>) for <a>football</a> in the <a>Southland Conference</a>.
<li>At home football games, large signs and banners representing campus fraternities, sororities, and student organizations adorn the grassy hills that surround Homer Bryce Stadium.</li>
In 2010, the two universities agreed to move the annual football matchups to <a>Houston, Texas</a> in <a>Reliant Stadium</a>.
<li>When the football team wins a home game, the purple beacons located on top of Steen Hall are turned on to light up the night</li>
<li>At home baseball games, the students in the outfield throw back opposing teams' home runs.</li>
