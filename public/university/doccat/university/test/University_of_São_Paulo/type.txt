There are campuses in the cities of <a>Bauru</a>, <a>Lorena</a>, <a>Piracicaba</a>, <a>Pirassununga</a>, <a>Ribeir&#x00e3;o Preto</a> and two in <a>S&#x00e3;o Carlos</a>.
<p>According to the 2010 <a>Academic Ranking of World Universities</a>, USP is placed in the group of the 101-151 top world universities.
The Polytechnic School includes <a>civil</a>, <a>electrical</a>, <a>mechanical</a>, <a>mining</a>, <a>metallurgical</a>, <a>naval</a> and <a>oceanic</a>, and <a>chemical engineering</a> departments.</p>
