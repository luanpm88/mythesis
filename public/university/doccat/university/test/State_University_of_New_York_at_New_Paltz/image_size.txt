The main campus has two dozen <a>academic</a> buildings, including the Haggerty Administration Building, a lecture hall, Old Main, <a>Sojourner Truth</a> Library, one main dining hall, a <a>Student Union</a> Building, and extensive <a>gym</a>nasium and sports areas.</p>
There is a 3:1 female to male ratio.</p>
