<p><a>thumb|Main building, around 1895.</a> Nine faculties with more than 150 first-degree full-time and part-time degree courses make the university the second largest higher education institution in <a>Lower Saxony</a>.
The university staff comprises 1,120 academic employees, including 340 professors, 1,560 employees in administrative functions, and some 900 additional staff funded by third-parties.</p>
