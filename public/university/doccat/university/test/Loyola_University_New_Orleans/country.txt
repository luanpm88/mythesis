Loyola is one of 28 member institutions that make up the <a>Association of Jesuit Colleges and Universities</a> and, with its current enrollment of approximately 5000 students, is among the larger Jesuit universities in the southern United States.
<p>Loyola is home to 5,000 students, including 3,000 undergraduates.

The Residence Hall Association, made up of residential students, serves as a representative for students to the administration and also allocates funding to student-run projects and activities.</p>
Loyola's Greek organizations are governed by three councils, the <a>Interfraternity Council</a>, the <a>Panhellenic Association</a>, and the <a>National Pan-Hellenic Council</a>.
In 2009, two members of Student Government Association's court were asked to resign due to their alleged membership in 1540.</p>
