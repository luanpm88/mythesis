<h4>College of Music and Fine Arts</h4>
The College of Music gives students the chance to combine liberal arts with professional music courses.
The college offers programs in Jazz Studies, Music Education, Music Therapy, Music Industry Studies, Instrumental Performance, Vocal Performance, Ballet, Theatre Arts, and Visual Arts.
The campus is located in the Greenville neighborhood, a former plantation and town annexed by New Orleans in the 19th Century.
