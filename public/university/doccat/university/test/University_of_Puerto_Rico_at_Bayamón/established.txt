<p> The <strong><a>University of Puerto Rico</a> at Bayam&#x00f3;n</strong> (<strong>UPRB</strong> or <strong>UPR-Bayam&#x00f3;n</strong>) is a <a>state university</a> located in the municipality of <a>Bayam&#x00f3;n</a>, <a>Puerto Rico</a>.
UPR-Bayam&#x00f3;n is better known as <strong>CUTB</strong> from its previous name of <em>Colegio Universitario Tecnol&#x00f3;gico de Bayam&#x00f3;n</em> in <a>Spanish</a>.
Also, it holds the second place in the dance team's competition.</p>
<p>The university has articulated transfer programs in <a>engineering</a>, <a>chemistry</a>, <a>social sciences</a> and <a>liberal arts</a>.
Also it has various <a>business administration</a> programs, accredited by <a>Association to Advance Collegiate Schools of Business</a>.
It hosts the only <a>Materials Management</a> degree in the system.
Also very popular is its Extended University program designed for students who for various reasons are unable to comply with a regular daytime schedule, as well as partnerships with businesses and industries in the area where students can do internships and practical programs before graduation .</p>
