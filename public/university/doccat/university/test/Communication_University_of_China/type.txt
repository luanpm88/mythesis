<p>The <strong>Communication University of China</strong>, CUC, (<a>Simplified Chinese</a>: &#x4e2d;&#x56fd;&#x4f20;&#x5a92;&#x5927;&#x5b66; <a>pinyin</a>: zh&#x014d;nggu&#x00f3; chu&#x00e1;nm&#x00e9;i d&#x00e0;xu&#x00e9;) is a public university in <a>Beijing</a>, <a>China</a>.
CUC is located in <a>Dingfuzhuang</a>, Chaoyang District just outside of the Eastern <a>5th Ring Road</a> in Eastern Beijing, and is just along the Beijing-Tong County expressway.</p>
