<p>Cal Maritime is the only campus in the CSU system that does not offer any graduate degree programs.</p>
Cal Maritime began to organize its sports under intercollegiate guidelines in the 1970s and the student body chose the &quot;Keelhaulers&quot; as the Academy's mascot.
Until then the Cal Maritime teams were known as the Seawolves.</p>
Other sports offered at Cal Maritime include Rugby, Sailing, Crew, and Water Polo.</p>
<p>Cal Maritime's Rugby team, despite the small size of the school, has been nationally ranked in club rugby and won the Pacific Coast League's Western Division Championship in 2009 and 2010.</p>
<p>The University's Sailing team captured the Kennedy Cup - the National Collegiate Sailing Championship - in the fall of 2009.
