As of 2008, the institute had an on campus student body of 6,400, almost equally divided between graduate- and undergraduate-level students, with the plurality of them focusing their studies on engineering and the sciences.
Enrollment Management covers undergraduate and graduate admissions as well as financial aid and scholarships office.
<p>As of October 2009, Florida Tech enrolled 2,654 undergraduate and 2,291 graduate students on its physical campuses and 4,731 undergraduate and 2,000 graduate students online.</li>
Some organizations are run by membership dues, such as the many fraternities and sororities on campus, as well as certain professional organizations like IEEE and AIAA.
