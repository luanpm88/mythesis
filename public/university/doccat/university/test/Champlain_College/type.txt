<p><strong>Champlain College</strong> is a private, <a>coeducation</a>al college located in <a>Burlington</a>, <a>Vermont</a>.
<p>About 60% of the students, 1,100, boarded at the college in 2009-10.</p>
Each course brings together 3 or 4 discrete disciplines with the use of various literature and open-ended discussion topics.
Through courses such as <a>Rhetoric</a>, Concepts of the Self, and Concepts of Community, students gain a background in the <a>liberal arts</a> and sciences.
<p>The college also offers a variety of programs online.
