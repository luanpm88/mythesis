Benedictine is one of a number of <a>U.S. Benedictine colleges</a>, and is sponsored by St. Benedict's Abbey and Mount St. Scholastica Monastery.
In 1970, Fr.
Alcuin Hemmen, OSB, president of St. Benedict's College, announced that St. Benedict's would become a co-educational college, causing Sr. Mary Noel Walter, OSB, president of Mount St. Scholastica College to organize discussion of a merger of the two colleges.
It continues to lease property from St. Benedict's College.</p>
The legend also has the raven saving St. Benedict from eating poisoned bread.</p>
