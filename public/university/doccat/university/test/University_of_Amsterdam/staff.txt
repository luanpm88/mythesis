With a budget of almost <a>&euro;</a>600 million, over 30,000 students and around 5,000 staff, the UvA is one of the major universities in <a>Europe</a>.
<p>Around 6500 students follow the courses on offer, and there are 800 staff members.</p>
The Faculty of Science consists of four departments with 1200 researchers and lecturers, including 150 full- and part-time professors operating in ten research institutes.
The main faculty buildings are located on the <a>Science Park Amsterdam</a>.</p>
