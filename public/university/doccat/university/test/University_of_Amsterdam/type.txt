There are seven faculties: <a>Humanities</a>, <a>Social</a> and <a>Behavioral sciences</a>, <a>Economy</a>, <a>Law</a>, <a>Science</a>, <a>Medicine</a> and <a>Dentistry</a>.
These world-class universities work together to invest in European 'centres of excellence', with the aim of maintaining Europe's strong position in the global knowledge economy.</p>
