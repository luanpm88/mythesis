Weber State University sports teams participate in <a>NCAA</a> <a>Division I</a> (<a>Football Championship Subdivision</a> for <a>football</a>) in the <a>Big Sky Conference</a>.
The Weber State University football team plays at <a>Stewart Stadium</a>.
<p>Weber State University athletics is composed of 15 different sporting teams and competes in the <a>Big Sky Conference</a>.
Their <a>mascot</a> is Waldo the Wildcat and team colors are purple and white, with black used as a number color.
All of the sporting teams compete in the <a>NCAA</a> <a>Division I</a> level.
The <a>football</a> team plays in the Division I &quot;Championship Subdivision&quot; formerly known as <a>Division 1-AA</a>.
The <a>football</a> team recently changed leadership, with the addition of new <a>head coach</a> <a>Ron McBride</a>, former <a>head coach</a> of the <a>University of Utah</a>, who began coaching the Wildcats in 2005.
Overall they finished 10-4 and lost in the NCAA Division I Football Championship Playoff Quarterfinals.</p>
For the 2005-2006 season, the Division I ice hockey team was ranked 6th nationally in the ACHA (American Collegiate Hockey Association).
