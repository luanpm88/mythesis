<p>Huaqiao University founded in 1960, with support from the late Chinese premier and historical figure <a>Zhou Enlai</a> for students of overseas Chinese backgrounds (<a>Hong Kong</a>, <a>Taiwan</a>, <a>Macau</a>, <a>Singapore</a>) to pursue tertiary education in their ancestral homelands.
The university has two campuses, one is the main campus in Quanzhou and the other one is the new campus in <a>Xiamen</a>.</p>
<h2>University History</h2>
<p>Since the founding of the university 45 years ago, the University has graduated more than 76,000 students, of which 36,000 are from overseas.
The University has now 24 000 full-time on-campus students, including 3,000 overseas students from 29 countries and regions such as Taiwan, Hong Kong, Macau, Malaysia, the Philippines, Indonesia, Thailand, Japan, United States and Argentina.</p>
<p>The men&#x2019;s basketball team of the University won the national championship in the 2nd, 5th and 7th and runner-up in the 4th CUBA (China University Basketball Association) tournaments.</p>
<h3>Chinese Language and Culture College</h3>
<p>Chinese Language and Culture College is subordinate to Huaqiao University, which used to be Remedial School of Jimei's overseas students (Jimei Chinese Language School), a special school to provide overseas students, foreign students with Chinese language and cultural knowledge.
Chinese Language and Culture College is the base of the national Chinese education, and the only place for HSK in Fujian Province instituted by the nation&#x2019;s HSK committee.
There are the short-term Chinese study course, training course of the Chinese teachers, overseas students&#x2019; summer/winter camp, the symposium of the Chinese culture and the preparatory course for the overseas Chinese and the youth students from <a>Hong Kong</a>, <a>Macau</a>, and <a>Taiwan</a>.
The library has a total collection of 2,040,000 volumes of 1,030,000 titles.</p>
<p>Since 1997, the university has enrolled a total of 216 post graduates from Macau, who pursued such fields as economy, enterprise management, computer science and philosophy.
The university has also offered <a>Master's degree</a>s to a total of 30 students from <a>Macau</a> in 2002.</p>
