<p>Several other administrative and research offices of Bocconi's individual institutes are scattered across the area, especially around Parco Ravizza and Viale Isonzo.</p>
The new building is now used to host all faculty staff offices and the research institutes of the university.</p>
<p>The <a>pelican</a> is Bocconi's official sport <a>mascot</a>.</p>
The central doors, which are larger than the others, have two lions to either side of them.
There is a superstition within the university that students who pass in-between the two lions via the central doors risk not being able to graduate.
