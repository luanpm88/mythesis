By virtue of being a major node in the European and global network of business and economics universities, Bocconi exchanges faculty and cooperates on large projects with like-minded European and North-American universities and business schools.
<p>Bocconi has close relations with major <a>corporation</a>s and international agencies, as well as their managers and officials, and constantly interacts with the business and economic environment to tackle new issues, implement new techniques, and start new research endeavours.</p>
Students in this course have the choice to major in <a>Economics</a>, <a>Management</a> or <a>Finance</a>.</p>
