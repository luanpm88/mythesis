<p> <a>right|thumb|Hebut</a> <strong>Hebei University of Technology</strong> (<strong>HEBUT</strong>; ) is a public university whose main campus lies in the Guangrong Dao neighborhood of the <a>Hongqiao</a> District in <a>Tianjin</a>, <a>China</a>.
It is the first <a>institute of technology</a> in modern Chinese education history.
It is among the first group of institutions of higher learning in the national &#x201c;<a>211-Project</a>&#x201d; to which priority is given in construction.
<h2>History</h2>
<p>The institution was established by <a>Yuan Shikai</a>, receiving a royal charter in 1903 as <a>Peiyang</a> Technological School, from <a>Guangxu Emperor</a>.
The university modeled itself on the famous European and American institutions of higher learning and aimed to rejuvenate China by training qualified personnel with new scientific and technological knowledge.
In 1958, after restructuring, institution was renamed <a>Hebei institution of Technology</a>.
