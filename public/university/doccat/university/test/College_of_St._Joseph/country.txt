In 1972, CSJP became a full member of the New England Association of Schools and Colleges, Inc.</p>
This program is unique in New England and one of a very few in the U.S. that seeks to make a higher education possible for former foster children.
Clubs and organizations on campus include Residence Hall Council, the Student Government Association, the Business Club, the Human Services Club, the Education Club, the Student National Education Association, the Outdoor Hiking Club, and the Drama Club.
