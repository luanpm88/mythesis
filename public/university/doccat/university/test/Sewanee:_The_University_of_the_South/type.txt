<p> <strong>The University of the South</strong> is a private, <a>coeducational</a> <a>liberal arts college</a> located in <a>Sewanee</a>, <a>Tennessee</a>.
Faculty and student members of the primary honor society and main branch of student government, the Order of Gownsmen, wear <a>academic gown</a>s to teach or attend class&#x2014;perhaps the last vestige of this historically <a>English</a> practice in North America.
