<p>The Clarkson School students are housed in <a>Farrisee</a> House in Price Hall and the typical class size is about 50 students.
All CUSA sponsored clubs are entirely student run and both undergrad and grad students are welcome to join any time.</p>
K2CC has both analog and digital voice repeaters and maintains a contest and experimentation room equipped with DX, week signal and satellite radios and antennas.</p>
