<p><strong>Lafayette College</strong> is a <a>private</a> <a>coeducation</a>al <a>liberal arts and engineering college</a> located in <a>Easton</a>, <a>Pennsylvania</a>, <a>USA</a>.
Lafayette's <a>endowment</a> is more than $680 million, placing it in the top 2% of all colleges and universities in endowment per student,  In <em><a>Kiplinger's Personal Finance</a></em> magazine, a study of the best valued schools ranked Lafayette 14th among liberal arts colleges.
Gilbert's Cafe is the site of many open mic nights where student bands can perform for their fellow classmates.</p>
Members of the club represent many disciplines in engineering and the liberal arts.
