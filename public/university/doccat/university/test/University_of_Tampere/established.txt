<p>  The <strong>University of Tampere</strong> () is a university in <a>Tampere</a>, <a>Finland</a>.
It was originally founded in 1925 in <a>Helsinki</a> as a Civic College (Kansalaiskorkeakoulu), and from 1930 onwards it was known as a School of Social Sciences (Yhteiskunnallinen korkeakoulu).
In 1960, the institution relocated to Tampere, and in 1966 it was officially named University of Tampere.</p>
