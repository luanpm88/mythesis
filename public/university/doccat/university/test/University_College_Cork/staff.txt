Student Centre with the O'Rahilly [[Arts]] and <a>Commerce</a> Building opposite]] Today the university has over 17,000 students - of which there are over 14,000 undergraduate degree candidates.
This student base is supported by 2,578 staff - of which 764 are faculty.
There are 1104 non academic staff and 710 research staff.</p>
The university's internal research reputation spans all of its faculties where it offers over 120 degree and professional programmes through seven schools and 27 departments.
