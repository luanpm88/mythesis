<p> <strong>Middle Tennessee State University</strong>, commonly abbreviated as <strong>MTSU</strong>, is a <a>public</a> <a>university</a> located in <a>Murfreesboro</a>, <a>Tennessee</a>.</p>
In 2009, Middle Tennessee State University was ranked among the top 100 public universities by Forbes.</p>
<p><a>thumb|left|1952 aerial photograph of the university</a> In 1909, the General Assembly of the State of <a>Tennessee</a> moved &quot;to provide for the improvement of the system of Public Education of the State of Tennessee, that is to say, to establish a General Education Fund.&quot;
<p>The Department of Aerospace offers an aerospace major with five concentrations: professional pilot, administration, technology, flight dispatch, and maintenance management.
Athletic facilities such as the <a>Murphy Center</a>, <a>Johnny &quot;Red&quot; Floyd Stadium</a>, the Alumni Memorial Gym, <a>Reese Smith Jr. Field</a>, and the university's tennis courts are also on the western part of campus.</p>
