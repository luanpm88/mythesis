<p><strong>Sichuan University</strong> (, often shortened simply to&quot;&#x5ddd;&#x5927;&quot;) is one of the oldest national <a>universities</a> in China.
It is ranked No. 8 among the Chinese universities according to the 2010 <a>Academic Ranking of World Universities</a>.</p>
<p>Sichuan University is one of the national universities directly under <a>the Ministry of Education (MOE)</a>.
It is also one of the State <a>211 Project</a> and <a>985 Project</a> universities enjoying privileged construction in the <a>Ninth Five-Year Plan</a> period.
<p> Sichuan University (SCU)  is the result of a merge of three national universities.
The 109 <a>bachelor's degree</a> programs SCU grants cover the main fields in <a>liberal arts</a>, <a>sciences</a>, <a>engineering</a>, <a>medicine</a> and <a>agriculture</a>.
The number of publications in science, engineering and medicine ranked sixth, and the citations ranked seventh among Chinese universities.
<p>Sichuan University's three campuses are located in <a>Chengdu</a>, the <a>capital city</a> of <a>Sichuan Province</a>.
