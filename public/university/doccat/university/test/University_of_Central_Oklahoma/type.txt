<p>In 1954, the Oklahoma State Regents for Higher Education gave Central permission to offer the Master of Teaching Degree, which became the <a>Master of Education</a> in 1969.
The Department of <a>Physics</a> is ranked among the top 20 in the United States and the only university in Oklahoma to receive this ranking by the <a>American Institute of Physics</a>.
