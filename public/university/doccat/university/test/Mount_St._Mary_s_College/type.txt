<p><strong>Mount St. Mary's College</strong> is a <a>private</a>, independent, <a>Catholic</a> <a>liberal arts college</a>, <a>primarily for women</a>, in <a>Los Angeles</a>, <a>California</a>.
<p>According to the 2007 <a>U.S. News &amp; World Report</a> listing of <em><a>America&#x2019;s Best Colleges</a></em>, Mount St. Mary&#x2019;s College is one of the highest ranked <a>master's</a>-granting universities in the Western United States, placing among the top twenty schools evaluated, out of 574 colleges and universities nationally.</p>
Usage of the abbreviated form of <em>Mount</em> or the complete form of <em>St.</em> is discouraged, and the possessive form <em>Mary's</em> (with an apostrophe) is required.</p>
