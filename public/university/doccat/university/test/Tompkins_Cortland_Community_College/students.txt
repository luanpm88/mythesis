The main college campus is located in the <a>Town of Dryden</a>.
The student body typically includes students from all parts of New York, a dozen other states, and more than 50 foreign countries.</p>
The men's soccer and golf programs and the women's soccer and softball programs are routinely nationally ranked.
Through a combination of transfer credits and study in the United States students may receive complementary degrees from their home university and TC3.
