<p><strong>Southern Adventist University</strong> is a <a>liberal arts</a> <a>university</a> located in <a>Collegedale, Tennessee</a>.
The university operates a <a>radio station</a>, <a>WSMC-FM</a>, which plays mainly <a>classical music</a> as well as <a>news</a> from the <a>BBC</a>, <a>National Public Radio</a>, and <a>Public Radio International</a>.
