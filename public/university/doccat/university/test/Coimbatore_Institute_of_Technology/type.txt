<p>The <strong>Coimbatore Institute of Technology</strong> (CIT) is one of the leading <a>DOTE</a> engineering colleges in <a>Tamil Nadu</a> located at <a>Coimbatore</a>, <a>India</a>.
It offers both <a>undergraduate</a> as well as <a>post-graduate</a> engineering courses.
<p>The Institute is located in one of the city's busiest road, <a>NH-47</a> ( Avinashi Road ).
