<p>The <strong>University of Puerto Rico at Arecibo</strong> (<strong>UPRA</strong> or <strong>UPR-Arecibo</strong>) is a <a>state university</a> located in the municipality of <a>Arecibo</a>, <a>Puerto Rico</a> and is part of the <a>University of Puerto Rico</a> system.
UPR-Arecibo is better known as <strong>CUTA</strong> from its previous name of Arecibo Technological University College <em>&quot;Colegio Universitario Tecnol&#x00f3;gico de Arecibo&quot;</em> in <a>Spanish</a>.</p>
<p>Associate and Baccalaureate Degrees offers among which stand out as being unique in the UPR system:</p>
<li>Bachelor of <a>Social Science</a> with concentration in <a>Industrial Organizational Psychology</a></li>
<li>Associate Degree in <a>Veterinary Technology</a>.</li>
<p>In addition, students in UPRA's Baccalaureate Program in <a>Elementary Education</a> highlighted by obtaining excellent scores on teacher certification exams of the <a>Puerto Rico Department of Education</a>.</p>
