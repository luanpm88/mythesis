The <a>management</a> and <a>human relations</a> degree is a non-traditional <a>undergraduate</a> degree geared toward working adults.
The <a>associate's degree</a>, the <a>master's degree</a>, and a <a>doctor of education</a> degree are also available.</p>
Events that have gained the most popularity among the student body would be Friday Night Live (a rendition of the popular skit show <a>Saturday Night Live</a>), Trojan Idol, and the Songwriter's Challenge.
