<p> <strong>Berry College</strong> is an <a>American</a> accredited, private, four-year <a>liberal arts college</a> located north of <a>Rome, Georgia</a>.
Designated portions are open to the public for hiking, cycling, horseback riding, and other outdoor activities.
There is also a wildlife refuge encompassing campus buildings and other public spaces where no hunting is allowed.</p>
According to <a>U.S. News and World Report</a> Berry is currently ranked 112th among the &quot;Top <a>Liberal Arts</a> colleges.&quot;</p>
<p>Minors can be obtained in 36 different courses of study throughout the four schools.</p>
