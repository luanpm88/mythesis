<p><strong>Beijing Normal University</strong> (), colloqiually known as &#x5317;&#x5e08;&#x5927; or  <strong>Beishida</strong>, is a public research university located in <a>Beijing</a>.
It is a national key university  and one of the oldest universities in China.</p>
It is located in northwest downtown Beijing in <a>Haidian</a> district, between the second and third ring roads, and is the closest among all Haidian universities to <a>Tiananmen Square</a>.</p>
