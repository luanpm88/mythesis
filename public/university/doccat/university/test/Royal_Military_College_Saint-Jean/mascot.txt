The courses that are offered at the centre are the Intermediate Leadership Qualification (ILQ), the Advanced Leadership Qualification (ALQ) and finally the CPO1/CWO Chief Qualification (CQ).
<p>The NCMPDC is a unique professional education establishment within the CF.
Upon successful completion of ROTP, Officer Cadets are awarded a university degree and granted commissions as Officers in the Canadian Forces.
The cadets take roles as cadet squadron commander, second in command (2IC) and section commanders.
