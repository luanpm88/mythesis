<p>The <strong>State University of New York at Delhi</strong> (also called <strong>SUNY Delhi</strong>) is one of the technology colleges of the <a>State University of New York</a> (SUNY) system.
The university, entirely in Delhi, is partially within the <a>Village of Delhi</a>.
