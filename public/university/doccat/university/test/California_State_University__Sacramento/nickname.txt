The football game is called the <a>Causeway Classic</a> and is played for the <a>Causeway Carriage</a>, referring to the fact that the schools are connected by the long <a>Yolo Causeway</a> bridge over <a>Yolo Bypass</a> floodway.
Male students compete in baseball, basketball, cross country, football, golf, soccer, tennis, and indoor and outdoor track and field.
