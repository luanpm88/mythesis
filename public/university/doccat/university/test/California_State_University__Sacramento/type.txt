<h3>Sacramento State Fight Song: &quot;<a>Fight, Hornet, Fight!</a>&quot;</h3>
<p>Sacramento State owns and operates multiple <a>public radio</a> stations throughout California in close cooperation with <a>Capital Public Radio</a>.
<p>Two of these stations are <a>KXPR</a> and <a>KXJZ</a>, both on <a>FM</a>.
<p>Both stations carry programming from <a>National Public Radio</a>.</p>
<p>Much is offered, including <a>fast food</a>, a game room, public <a>computer</a>s with <a>internet</a> access, free <a>WiFi</a>, and <a>conference room</a>s.</p>
A Sacramento State student can use these resources for free with their student one card.</p>
