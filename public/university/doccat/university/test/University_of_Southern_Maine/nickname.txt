The Gorham campus, much more residential, is home to the College of Education and the School of Music.
USM is a major educational engine in the overall growth and development of economic, civic, and cultural life in southern <a>Maine</a>.
Gorham is home to most of the University's dormitories and competitive athletic facilities.
The primary academic areas residing in Gorham are industrial technologies, engineering, art, music, theater, counseling and education, anthropology, geography, environmental sciences, and geosciences.</p>
Master of Arts in Leadership and Master of Occupational Therapy degrees are offered exclusively at Lewiston-Auburn College.
