<h2>History</h2>
<p>Handan College established in December, 1905, has been operating in the higher education for over 60 years.
The college has endured several historical periods from the end of <a>Qing Dynasty</a> to the foundation of the <a>People&#x2019;s Republic of China</a>.
Thus, the education policies of each period have imprinted their influences on its development from a primary normal school of Handan County to a junior senior normal school and to a university of Handan City.
<p>The library is equipped with 595,000 books and a digital library and advanced equipments and technology of multi-media and computer network have been used widely in teaching and scientific research.
So the campus looks harmonious and elegant, simple and quiet, bathed in a thick cultural and academic atmosphere.</p>
<p>In the last 60 years, 30,000 students have graduated from Handan College.
