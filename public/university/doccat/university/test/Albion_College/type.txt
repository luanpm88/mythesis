<p> <strong>Albion College</strong> is a private <a>liberal arts college</a> located in <a>Albion, Michigan</a>.
These include the Prentiss M. Brown Honors program, The Center for Sustainability and the Environment, Fritz Shurmur Education Institute, the <a>Gerald R. Ford Institute for Public Policy and Service at Albion College</a>, the Carl A. Gerstacker Institute for Professional Management, and pre-professional programs in <a>engineering</a>, <a>medicine</a>, and <a>law</a>.</p>
Programs are offered in <a>Philadelphia</a>, <a>Chicago</a>, <a>London</a>, <a>Heidelberg</a>, <a>T&#x00fc;bingen</a>, <a>Tokyo</a>, <a>Seoul</a>, <a>Cape Town</a>, <a>Aix-en-Provence</a>, <a>Ballyvaughan</a>, <a>Athens</a>, <a>Brussels</a> and <a>Paris</a>.</p>
They include <a>Alpha Lambda Delta</a>, <a>Alpha Phi Omega</a>, <a>Delta Sigma Pi</a>, <a>Sigma Xi</a>, <a>Theta Phi Alpha</a>, <a>Kappa Kappa Psi</a>, <a>Omicron Delta Kappa</a>, <a>Order of Omega</a>, <a>Phi Beta Kappa</a>, <a>Phi Mu Alpha Sinfonia</a>, <a>Pi Sigma Alpha</a>, <a>Psi Chi</a>, <a>Sigma Alpha Iota</a>, and <a>Sigma Gamma Epsilon</a>.</p>
