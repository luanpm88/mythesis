<p>  The <strong>Ocean University of China</strong> (<a>simplified Chinese</a>: &#x4e2d;&#x56fd;&#x6d77;&#x6d0b;&#x5927;&#x5b66;; pinyin: Zh&#x014d;nggu&#x00f3; Ha&#x00ec;y&#x00e1;ng D&#x00e0;xu&#x00e9;, local: &#x6d77;&#x5927; or Ha&#x00ec;d&#x00e0;), located in <a>Qingdao</a>, is one of the key comprehensive <a>universities</a> under the direct jurisdiction of the Ministry of Education of the <a>People's Republic of China</a>.
It consists of such branches of learning as <a>science</a>, <a>engineering</a>, <a>agronomy</a> (fishery), <a>economics</a>, <a>literature</a>, <a>medical science</a>(pharmaceutics), <a>philosophy</a> and <a>law</a>.
