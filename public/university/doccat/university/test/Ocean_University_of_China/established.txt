<h2>History</h2>
<p>The university was first set up in 1924 as a private institution, and with the founding of <a>Shandong College of Oceanology</a> (&#x5c71;&#x4e1c;&#x6d77;&#x6d0b;&#x5b66;&#x9662;) in 1959, became one of the thirteen national key universities in October 1960.
In January 1988, it was renamed Ocean University of Qingdao, and <a>Deng Xiaoping</a> wrote the name of the university in his own <a>calligraphic</a> style to mark the occasion.
In October 2002, it was renamed Ocean University of China with the authorization from the Ministry of Education, People's Republic of China.</p>
<p>The university's main campus is situated on a hill with a view of the sea in a scenic area of <a>Qingdao</a>, a coastal city with a temperate climate, in <a>Shandong</a> province.</p>
<p>With its main campus (Yushan, considered the most beautiful one) in <a>Shinan District</a> and the sub-campus Fushan in the <a>Laoshan District</a>, the university covers an area of more than 733,000&nbsp;m&#x00b2; with a floor space of 230,000&nbsp;m&#x00b2;.</p>
<p>A new larger campus named Laoshan in the Laoshan District was opened in the fall of 2006.</p>

<p>Ocean University of China is one of the first universities authorized by the <a>State Council</a>'s Academic Degree Committee to confer doctoral, master and bachelor degrees.
Seven key research labs affiliated with <a>Ministry of Education of the People's Republic of China</a>, nine key research labs affiliated with <a>Shandong Province</a>, and three key research labs affiliated with <a>Qingdao</a> Municipality have been set up in the university.
The Qingdao Marine Science and Technology National Laboratory, headed by OUC, with an estimated total investment of one billion <a>Chinese yuan</a>, is currently under construction.
The university has also set up a national base for training talents to be engaged in basic research and teaching in the fields of oceanology and marine chemistry.
Among them are four academicians of the <a>Chinese Academy of Sciences</a>, three academician of the <a>Chinese Academy of Engineering</a> and more than 250 Ph.D. supervisors.
101 professors enjoy special subsidies from the <a>State Council</a>, 9 professors have been awarded the title of Middle-aged and Young Experts Who Have Made Outstanding Contributions to the State.
Mr. <a>Wang Meng</a>, a famous modern Chinese writer, is a professor at OUC, and also an Honorary Dean of the College of Literature, Journalism and Communication.. </p>
<p>The university's objectives are to increase reform in education and teaching, continue to focus on its specialties and to meet the need of social and economic development.
Under the school guideline of &quot;Unity, Diligence, Practicality and Creativity&quot;, the university has cultivated a unique educational style and school discipline.
Strict teaching and administration have steadily raised the quality of education.
The series of measures to reform and promote teaching and learning in the university have won appraisal and support from both teachers and students of the school and from the society at large.</p>
The school <a>library</a> has a collection of 800,000 books and more than 2000 Chinese and foreign journals and periodicals.
<a>Journal of Ocean University China</a> (both natural science and social science editions) is a regular publication of the university.
The campus computer network with 3,000 user-terminals connected with <a>internet</a> and <a>Cernet</a> has been set up.</p>
<p>The university has set up 26 research institutions, among which the <a>Academy of Marine Drugs</a>, the <a>Institute of Physical Oceanography</a>, the institute of <a>Ocean Remote Sensing</a>, and the <a>Academy of Marine Economy</a> and the <a>Law of the Sea</a> are most well-known.
Since the beginning of the <a>Eighth Five-Year Plan</a> period, 65 of the school's research achievements have reached the international leading or international advanced level, 85 have been considered to be original or advanced in China, and 149 monographs and 3028 academic papers have been published.
Besides, the university has got 105 national patents and 4 of the research results have been registered for patents in foreign countries.</p>
<p>The university has always laid stress on the training of its teachers and the promotion of foreign academic exchanges.
In the past years, half of the teachers of the university has been to foreign countries as students, visiting scholars or guest lecturers.
It has established cooperative and exchange relationship with more than 50 universities and research institutions in 35 foreign countries or regions.</p>
<p>In 1994, the university began to operate under the dual leadership of <a>State Education Commission</a> and the <a>People's Government of Shandong Province</a>.
Successfully passing the strict preliminary assessment and examination for the Project-211 in January 1996 and getting admitted into the project in June 1997, Ocean University of China has entered a new stage of development.</p>
