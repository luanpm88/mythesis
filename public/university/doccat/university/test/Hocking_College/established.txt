<p><strong>Hocking College</strong>, a two-year <a>technical college</a> located in <a>Nelsonville, Ohio</a>, in the <a>Appalachian</a> Mid-West Region of the <a>United States</a> offers a wide selection of curriculum, including two year and four year degrees, as well as several certification programs.</p>
<h2>History</h2>
<p>The College was founded in 1968.
<p>In August 2008, the Ohio State Auditors website announced an audit of Hocking College to explore &quot;various allegations regarding certain activities and financial transactions at the college.&quot;
<p>In April 2009, it was announced in the wake of a criminal investigation that college president John Light would be replaced by Dr. Ron Erickson of Minnesota.
The Audit report shows that this money should be paid back to the college and criminal prosecution is impending a final investigation by the State of Ohio's Ethics Commission.
<p>In January 2010, the <a>FBI</a> began investigating a hate crime, when a note was found written on a bathroom wall that threatened that black students would be killed on February 2. A racist note was found a short time later.</p>
