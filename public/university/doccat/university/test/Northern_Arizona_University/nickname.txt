In 1966, the Arizona Board of Regents recommended the name be changed to the current incarnation, Northern Arizona University.
<p><a>right|thumb|Northern Arizona Lumberjacks logo</a> Student athletes go on to compete at national, international, and professional levels in football, basketball, baseball, ice hockey, track and field, tennis, swimming and diving.
<p>The <a>Lumberjack</a>s compete at the <a>NCAA Division I</a> level in all sports.
In football, the Lumberjacks compete at the <a>Football Championship Subdivision</a> (formerly known as Division I-AA) level.
<h2>On Campus Activities/Student Media</h2>
<p>Intramural sports are organized for teams and individuals and include flag football, soccer, volleyball, softball, racquetball, and backgammon.
