<p>The UAH Propulsion Research Center ([PRC]) is a research center that promotes interdisciplinary research opportunities for graduate and undergraduate students.
<h2>Athletics</h2>
With the exceptions of ACE and the campus newspaper <em>The Exponent</em>, all student organizations must be chartered with the SGA.
The SGA also charters two buses to allow approximately 80 UAH students to make an annual trip to <a>Niagara, New York</a> to cheer for the UAH Ice Hockey team as they compete against <a>Niagara University</a>.</p>
<p><em>The Exponent</em> is the name of UAH's newspaper, which is printed weekly and is available at locations around campus free of charge.</p>
