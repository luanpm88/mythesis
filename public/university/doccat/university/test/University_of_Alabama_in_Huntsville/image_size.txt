<h2>Student life and activities</h2>
<p>UAH has five residence halls:  Central Campus Residence Hall (CCRH), Frank Franz Hall, North Campus Residence Hall, Southeast Campus Housing, and Charger Village.
<h3>Student Success Center</h3>
<p>The Student Success Center (SSC) (formerly known as the Academic Resource Center (ARC)) offers tutoring for nearly all freshman and sophomore level courses offered at UAH.
