<h3>Sports and athletic facilities</h3>
<p>Students participate in intramural, extramural and the <em>Tamil Nadu Inter Engineering Sports </em>(TIES) competitions.
<p>&lt;center&gt;&lt;/center&gt;</p>
Buses are operated on Saturdays and Sundays for special classes, <a>NCC</a>, Sports etc.
Other notable awards include the Outstanding Undergraduate student Award, Outstanding Sports person Award and the Best Student Award by the Indian Society for Technical Education.
