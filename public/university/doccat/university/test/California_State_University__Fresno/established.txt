<p><strong>California State University, Fresno</strong>, unofficially referred to as <strong>Fresno State University</strong> and synonymously known as <strong>Fresno State</strong>, is one of the campuses of <a>California State University</a> system, located at the northeast edge of <a>Fresno</a>, <a>California</a>, <a>USA</a>.</p>
<p>The campus sits at the foot of the <a>Sierra Nevada</a> mountain range in the <a>San Joaquin Valley</a>.
The university is within an hour's drive of many mountain and lake resorts and within a three hour drive of both <a>Los Angeles</a> and <a>San Francisco</a>.</p>
<p>Fresno State operates on a <a>semester</a> schedule.
The spring semester runs from mid-January through mid-May.</p>
