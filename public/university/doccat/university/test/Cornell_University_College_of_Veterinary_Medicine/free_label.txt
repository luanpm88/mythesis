Cornell's other co-founder and first president, <a>Andrew Dickson White</a>, was asked by Ezra Cornell to find the best-qualified person to teach veterinary-related courses at the university.
<p>As the <a>cooperative extension</a> service for New York State, veterinary faculty and extension staff facilitate outreach programs for the community and State of New York, conduct research, and perform services involving disease prevention, production quality control, food and farm safety and animal well-being.
<p>Cornell's Animal Health Diagnostic Center, located on the Ithaca campus, has been a leader in animal health research for decades.
