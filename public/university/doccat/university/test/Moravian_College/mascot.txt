<p>Art and music programs are offered in Bethlehem's historic district on the college's South Campus, also known as the Church Street Campus and now the Priscilla Payne Hurd Campus.
Students who want to get up to North Campus can get the Moravian shuttle right outside the door.
<p>Located at one of the two quads of the North Campus are the soccer and lacrosse field.
The Bethlehem Steel Soccer Club continued to use the field until its demise.
