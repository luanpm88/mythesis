<h2>Short courses</h2>
Over 180 different short courses are offered.
These short courses run all of the College's 6 sites across London.
<li>E-Learning] (short courses delivered online via virtual lectures and chat sessions)</li>
<p>E-learning short courses are a way of studying an LCF short courses without attending classes in London.
Students attend weekly pre-arranged 'virtual lectures'.
A total of 8 different e-learning short courses are currently offered.
