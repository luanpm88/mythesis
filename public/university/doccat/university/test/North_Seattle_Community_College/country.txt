It is one of the three colleges comprising the <a>Seattle Community College District</a> (the others are <a>Seattle Central Community College</a> and <a>South Seattle Community College</a>), and one of the 32 member colleges of the <a>Washington Community and Technical Colleges</a> system.
NSCC is also home to the Watch Technology Institute, the only two-year program in the art of Swiss <a>watchmaking</a> and repair in the <a>Western United States</a>.</p>
