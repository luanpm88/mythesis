<h2>History</h2>
<p>The Universidad Cient&#x00ed;fica del Sur was founded in 1996 by a Promoting Association led by Jos&#x00e9; Carlos Dextre Chac&#x00f3;n and an Organizing Commission led by Fernando Cabieses.</p>
<p>Two years later, the National Commission on the Functioning of Universities (CONAFU) approved the temporary activity of UCSUR by Resoluci&#x00f3;n N&#x00b0;356-98-CONAFU.
<p>The first official ceremony took place the 26th of March, 1998, in Museo de Osma.
The admission campaign started in July.
The Campus was originally in <a>Surco</a> district.
However, a few years later, it was moved to its current location, close to the <a>Pantanos de Villa Reserved Zone</a>.</p>
<p>In March 2006, CONAFU awarded the <a>Definitive Authorization Resolution</a> to the university, which implies institutional autonomy.
<p>A board is in charge of the university's administration.
Among several functions, the board is responsible of appointing the <a>Rector</a> and the <a>Dean</a> of faculty.
Also, it coordinates with the Rector's office to take care of financial and academic issues.</p>
