<p> <strong>Southeast Missouri State University</strong>, is a public, accredited <a>university</a> located in <a>Cape Girardeau, Missouri</a>, <a>United States</a>, near the banks of the <a>Mississippi River</a>.
Today it houses the Southeast Missouri Public Radio Studio, as well as numerous classrooms, workshops, and photography and drafting Studios.
In spring 2005, Southeast eliminated Three Rivers courses from those centers, citing failure of the community college to pay approximately $10,000 in facilities-use fees.
