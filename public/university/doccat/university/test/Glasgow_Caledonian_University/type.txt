<p> <strong>Glasgow Caledonian University</strong> is a <a>public university</a> in <a>Glasgow</a>, <a>Scotland</a>.</p>
<li><a>Health</a>;</li>
It is also home to a number of <a>research</a> centres such as the Scottish <a>Poverty</a> Information Unit, Caledonian Heritage Futures Network and the Centre for <a>Equality</a> and <a>Diversity</a>.
<p>Glasgow Caledonian University offers programmes in all of the Scottish Higher Education Funding Council (SHEFC) funding groups except <a>medicine</a>, <a>dentistry</a> and <a>teacher</a> education.
Fitness classes run seven days a week and the Arc is open to not just the students but the entire public.</p>
