<p><strong>Westminster College</strong> is a private <a>liberal arts college</a> located in the <a>Sugar House</a> neighborhood of <a>Salt Lake City, Utah</a>.
Peterson's Guide to Competitive Colleges includes Westminster College in the top 10 percent of 3,600 public and private colleges and universities nationwide.
<p>Westminster College is a comprehensive college blending liberal arts studies with professional programs.
