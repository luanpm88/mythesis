<p>  The <strong>Ibero-American University</strong> (in <a>Spanish</a>: <strong><em>Universidad Iberoamericana</em></strong>, abbreviated <strong><em>UIA</em></strong> but commonly known as <strong><em>Ibero</em></strong>) is a <a>Mexican</a> private institution of <a>higher education</a> sponsored by the <a>Society of Jesus</a>.
<p>Its main library, <em><a>Biblioteca Francisco Xavier Clavigero</a></em>, holds more than 250,000 books and journals.
It is one of the largest law libraries in Mexico and as of 2007, it is one of the largest university libraries in the country.</p>
