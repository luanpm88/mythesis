<p><strong>Vilnius Gediminas Technical University</strong> ( acronym-<strong>VGTU</strong>) is one of the two technical universities in <a>Lithuania</a>.</p>
<h2>History</h2>
<p>September 1, 1956 is considered to be the date of founding a higher technical school in <a>Vilnius</a>, after the Vilnius Evening division of the Evening faculty of <a>Kaunas Polytechnic Institute</a> (KPI) was established.
<p>6 July 1961 the KPI Vilnius Evening Faculty was restructured into KPI Vilnius Branch, with 2 divisions: the day-time and evening studies.
KPI Vilnius Branch was restructured into the <strong>Vilnius Building and Engineering Institute</strong> (VISI)).
<p>In 1990 the Vilnius Building and Engineering Institute became Vilnius Technical University, which on August 22, 1996 was awarded the name of <a>Gediminas</a>, the <a>Grand Duke of Lithuania</a>.</p>
