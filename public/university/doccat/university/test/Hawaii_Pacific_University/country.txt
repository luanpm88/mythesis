<p>In 1973, the College received full accreditation from the Western Association of Schools and Colleges.
A significant online program is also offered.
<p>HPU also has a highly acclaimed and diverse Model United Nations program with students from Myanmar, Canada, Pakistan, Germany, and the United States.
They have earned recognition for their efforts by earning an Excellent Delegation ranking at the National Model United Nations program in New York, New York.</p>
