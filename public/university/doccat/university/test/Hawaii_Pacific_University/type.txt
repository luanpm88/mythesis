<p><strong>Hawai<img>i Pacific University</strong>, also known as <strong>HPU</strong>, is a <a>private</a>, <a>Nonsectarian</a>, <a>coeducational</a> university located in <a>Honolulu, Hawaii</a> and <a>Kaneohe, Hawaii</a>.
<p>In 1992, <a>Hawaii Loa College</a>, a small, independent, liberal arts college located on the windward side of O<img>ahu, merged into Hawai<img>i Pacific University.
<p>HPU's <a>downtown Honolulu</a> campus serves most of the business, liberal arts, and other general programs.
