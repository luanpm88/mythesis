<p>The <strong>University of Michigan&#x2013;Dearborn</strong> (commonly referred to as <strong>UM-Dearborn</strong> or <strong>UM-D</strong>), is a public university located in <a>Dearborn, Michigan</a>, <a>USA</a>.
It is ranked as the 4th best public masters's university in the Midwest.</p>
According to the U.S. News &amp; World Report's 2009 America's Best Colleges review, the University of Michigan&#x2013;Dearborn is rated the 28th best master's-level university in the <a>Midwest</a> (overall), and 4th best public master's-level university in the Midwest.
<p>CASL programs are designed to be a liberal arts education reflective of the needs of the metropolitan Detroit region.
Public spaces, such as the entrance rotunda, student break areas, and classroom corridors, have been designed to spaciously make use of natural lighting.
<h3>School of Education</h3>
<p><a>The School of Education (SOE) </a> offers undergraduate, master's, and one doctoral program to students.
