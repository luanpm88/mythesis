<p><strong>B.M Sreenivasaiah College of Engineering</strong> (also <strong>B.M.S College of Engineering</strong> or <strong>BMSCE</strong>) is an engineering and management college in <a>Bangalore</a>, <a>India</a>.
The engineering college, started in 1946, was one of the first private sector initiatives in technical education in India.
It is located on the Bull Temple Road, opposite to the <a>Bull Temple</a>.
One of the oldest engineering colleges in Karnataka, it celebrated its &quot;Diamond Jubilee&quot; in 2006.
It is also known for the annual inter-collegiate Techno-Cultural festival UTSAV, one of the prominent student-managed festivals in the country.</p>
