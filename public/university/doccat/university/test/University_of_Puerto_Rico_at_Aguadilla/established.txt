<p>  The <strong>University of Puerto Rico at Aguadilla</strong> (<strong>UPRAG</strong> or <strong>UPR-Aguadilla</strong>) is a <a>state university</a> located in the city of <a>Aguadilla</a>, <a>Puerto Rico</a>.</p>
<p>Located in northwestern Puerto Rico, the University of Puerto Rico at Aguadilla (UPRAg) offer Associate and Baccalaureate Degrees among which stand out as being unique in the UPR system:</p>
<li>Bachelor of Arts in <a>Education</a> with a concentration in English and multimedia technology.</li>
<li>Bachelor in <a>Business Administration</a> with concentrations in <a>Human Resources</a>, <a>Computerized Information Systems</a>, <a>Accounting</a>, <a>Marketing</a> and <a>Finances</a>.</li>
<li>Bachelor of Science in <a>Biology</a> with emphasis in Bioinformatics, Biomedical Quality Systems Assessment Process Industries.</li>
<p>It also has academic programs and continuing education activities and programs aimed at high school at night and alternate schedules, as well as a comprehensive platform to offer online courses.</p>
