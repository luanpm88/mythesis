<p>The school colors of Agnes Scott are purple and white, while the mascot is Irvine, a <a>Scottish Terrier</a>.
The colors and mascots are intended to establish class pride, particularly during one week of activities called Black Cat.
<p>Tradition dictates that students who get engaged are thrown into the alumnae pond by their classmates.</p>
