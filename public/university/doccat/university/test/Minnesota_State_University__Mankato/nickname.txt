The athletic teams are known as the Minnesota State University Mavericks with school colors of purple and gold.
Teams primarily compete at the <a>NCAA</a> <a>Division II</a> level in the <a>Northern Sun Intercollegiate Conference</a>.
The exceptions are the Men's and Women's <a>ice hockey</a> teams who compete at the <a>NCAA</a> <a>Division I</a> level, in the highly regarded <a>Western Collegiate Hockey Association</a> (WCHA).
