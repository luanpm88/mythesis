<p> <strong>The University of Crete</strong> <strong>(UOC)</strong> is the principal higher education institution on the island of <a>Crete</a>, <a>Greece</a>.</p>
<p>The University of Crete, is a multi-disciplinary, research- oriented institution, located in the cities of Rethymnon and <a>Heraklion</a>.
It is a University with a well-known reputation both nationally and internationally for its state-of-the art curricula and graduate programmes In addition, the University has developed considerable research activity and has undertaken innovative initiatives that reflect its dynamic.
The <a>University</a> was established in 1973 and started functioning in the academic year 1977-78.
As a higher education institution, it is a legal person of public law, i.e. it operates under the supervision of the State.
The seat of the University is in <a>Rethymnon</a>.</p>
<p>The supreme administrative body of the University is the senate, which is presided over by the <a>Rector</a> of the University.
The Head of the University is the Rector who is assisted by three Vice-Rectors in the exercise of his duties; all four together form the institution's high authority.
<p>The University of Crete has 13,141 students (11,011 at the <a>undergraduate</a> level and 2,130 at the <a>postgraduate</a> level), more than 900 Faculty members and researchers as well as approximately 240 administrative staff, in Schools and their Departments in the cities of <a>Rethymnon</a> and <a>Heraklion</a>.
<p>At <a>Rethymnon</a>, the School of Letters comprises the Departments of <a>History</a>, <a>Archaeology</a>, <a>Philosophy</a> and <a>Social Studies</a>, and <a>Philology</a>.
<p>At <a>Heraklion</a>, the School of Sciences and Engineering includes the Departments of <a>Biology</a>, <a>Chemistry</a>, <a>Computer Science</a>, <a>Mathematics</a>, <a>Applied Mathematics</a>, <a>Materials Science</a> and Technology, and <a>Physics</a>, while the School of Health Sciences includes the faculty of <a>Medicine</a>.
The <a>Natural History</a> Museum of Crete, established in 1981 at <a>Heraklion</a>, is also part of the University of Crete.</p>
<p>Although still very young, the University of Crete has already shown its commitment in the evolving European process.
<p>The University of Crete is  reputed to be the best research university in Greece.</p>
