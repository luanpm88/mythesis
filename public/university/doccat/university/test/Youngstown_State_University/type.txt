It features reading and study rooms, computer labs, a copying center, YSU's bookstore, a variety of restaurants (including a full-service <a>Arby's</a> and a <a>KFC Express</a>), and many student-affairs offices.
<p>YSU is also known as the one of the few universities to have <a>college professors</a>, rather than <a>graduate assistants</a>, directly teach undergraduate students, as it is more common at large universities to use graduate assistants.
Each of the Departments offers strong educational opportunities and most offer many research and service activities to their students.
Such contributions have been growing rapidly such that many of the departments, (e.g., English, Chemistry, Mathematics &amp; Statistics) are at or near the top of national rankings for departments of their type.
