<p>Its main campus is located in the <a>Bar&#x00e3;o Geraldo</a> district, 6 miles (10&nbsp;km) away from downtown <a>Campinas</a>, with additional campuses in <a>Limeira</a> and <a>Piracicaba</a>.</p>
<p>Unicamp's <a>teaching hospital</a>, <a>Hospital de Cl&#x00ed;nicas</a>, is the largest public hospital in the region.
<p>According to the <a>Times Higher Education 2007 World University Rankings</a>, Unicamp is the 177th best university in the world, and the 2nd best in Latin America (after the <a>University of S&#x00e3;o Paulo</a> in 176th place).</p>
