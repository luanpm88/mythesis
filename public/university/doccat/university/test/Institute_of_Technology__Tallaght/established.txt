<p><strong>Institute of Technology, Tallaght</strong> (<strong>ITT</strong>) formerly <strong>Regional Technical College, Tallaght</strong>, located in <a>Tallaght</a>, <a>South Dublin</a> in <a>Ireland</a>.</p>
<h2>History</h2>
<p>The institute was established in 1992, at that time the final new <a>Regional Technical College</a> opened in Ireland.</p>
<p>In 2005 the institute adopted the abbreviated title of <strong>ITT Dublin</strong>, to differentiate it from IT Tralee; this does not change the legal name which continues to be &quot;Institute of Technology, Tallaght&quot; as confirmed in the Institutes of Technology Act, 2006.</p>
ITT is in the process of redeveloping land donated by Saint Mary's Priory for use as sports pitches.</p>
His attitude towards college focuses on a healthy social/academic balance and has always noted that some of the best sports men and women from the college have also been the top performers in the classroom.
It is unequivocally Tim who keeps the blood of IT Tallaght's sports and societies flowing strongly.</p>
<p>The institute is the <a>hub</a> of the <a>ITnet</a> <a>network</a>.</p>
