<p><strong>China Academy of Art</strong> () is the highest university of art in <a>China</a>.
It was founded in <a>Hangzhou</a> in 1928 by the <a>government</a> of <a>Republic of China</a>.</p>
<p><strong>China Academy of Art</strong> (also <strong>China National Academy of Fine Arts</strong>), colloquially known in Chinese as <strong>Guomei</strong> (&#x570b;&#x7f8e;, <em>Gu&#x00f3;m&#x011b;i</em>).</p>
<p>The China Academy of Art has the most complete range of degree offerings and programs of study in fine arts in China.
The administration of the Academy is headquartered in the Nanshan Campus, which is located right on the beautiful West Lake in Hangzhou.
Students in the Master's program live on the alternate campus 40 minutes away, on the outskirts of <a>Hangzhou</a>.
A free bus is offered to transport students to and from the campus from the dorms at designated times.
Taxis do not drive to the outlying campus after 4:30 in Hangzhou.
<p>Undergraduate students typically spend one year in general education courses in the Department of Foundation and then three years in the core courses of their particular majors.
<p>The school is not run in western measures, meaning it is the student's job, not the faculty to orientate around the school.
It is the students responsibility to locate learning materials, living amenities, and departments such as the financial office.
<p>China Academy's pedagogy consists mostly of mimicking techniques of former Chinese artists rather than appropriating to one's own style.
China Academy encourages communistic unity in scaffolding techniques, rather than by western <a>Montessori method</a>s.
<p>The Academy encourages diversity in thinking, art exploration and academy research.
It takes as its motto &#x201c;diversity in harmony&#x201d; and fosters an environment for innovation.</p>
<h2>History</h2>
<p>In 1928, the National Academy of Art(&#x570b;&#x7acb;&#x85dd;&#x8853;&#x9662;) was founded as the first comprehensive art academy in China on the bank of the <a>West Lake</a> in <a>Hangzhou</a> by the eminent educator <a>Cai Yuanpei</a>(&#x8521;&#x5143;&#x57f9;) with Mr. <a>Lin Fengmian</a>(&#x6797;&#x98a8;&#x7720;).
It takes integrating eastern and western arts, creating arts of the time and developing Chinese culture as its tenets.
