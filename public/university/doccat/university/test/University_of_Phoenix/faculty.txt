<p>University of Phoenix has an <a>open enrollment</a> admission policy other than requiring a high-school diploma, <a>GED</a>, or its equivalent.
Available, for example, are virtual companies created by the university to provide students with assignments, which Adam Honea, UOPX's <a>dean</a> and <a>provost</a>, claims are more realistic than those available with <a>case studies</a>.</p>
Each learning team is assigned a team forum where team members will discuss the project and submit their agreed upon portions of the learning team assignment for compilation by the nominated learning team leader.
