<p>The <strong>Polytechnic University of Puerto Rico</strong> (<strong>PUPR</strong>) &mdash;commonly referred as <strong>Poly</strong> or <strong>La Poly</strong> in <a>Spanish</a>&mdash; is a <a>private</a> <a>non-profit</a> <a>university</a> located in <a>San Juan</a>, <a>Puerto Rico</a>.
PUPR offers a broad range of technical degrees; specializing in <a>engineering</a> professions.</p>
The main campus is located in the island's financial district which is the very core of metropolitan <a>San Juan</a>.
