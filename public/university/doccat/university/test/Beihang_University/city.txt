In recent years BUAA has ranked among the foremost in China in terms of funding for scientific research.
<p>BUAA was among the nation' s first 16 key universities back in the 1950' s, and among the top 15 for priority development during China' s 7th five-year-plan period (1986&#x2013;1990).
During the 8th five-year-plan it was listed among the top 14 for priority development, and at the start of the 9th five-year-plan BUAA was among the first batch of 15 universities in &quot;project 211 for Higher Education.&quot;
