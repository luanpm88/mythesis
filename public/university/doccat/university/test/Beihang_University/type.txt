<p>   <strong>Beihang University</strong>, previously known as <strong>Beijing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics</strong> (, abbreviated as <em>BUAA</em> or <em>Beihang</em>) is a <a>public university</a> located in <a>Beijing</a>, <a>China</a>.</p>
BUAA is home of 23,000 undergrad and graduate students.
<p>In 2005, French leading <a>Grande &#x00c9;cole</a> of engineering &quot;<a>&#x00c9;cole Centrale</a>&quot; associated with BUAA to create a top level engineering graduate program called <a>&#x00c9;cole Centrale de P&#x00e9;kin</a>.
BUAA is among the very first academic institutes established graduate schools in China and it is also one of the universities which have the authority to approve advisors for doctoral programs.
