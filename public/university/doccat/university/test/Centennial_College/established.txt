<p><strong>Centennial College of Applied Arts and Technology</strong> is the oldest publicly funded <a>community college</a> in <a>Ontario</a>, founded in <a>1966</a>.
The enabling legislation is the <em>Ministry of Training, Colleges and Universities Act</em>  Centennial College is recognized as one of the most culturally diverse post-secondary institutions in Ontario.</p>
