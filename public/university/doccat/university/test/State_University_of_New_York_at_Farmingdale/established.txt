<p>The <strong>Farmingdale State College</strong>, also known as <strong>SUNY Farmingdale</strong>, and also called <strong>Farmingdale State College</strong>, is the former <strong>Long Island</strong> <strong>Agricultural and Technical Institute or LIATI.</strong>
The college dates from 1912 as a school of applied agriculture.
Currently the college offers bachelor degrees and associate degrees across various fields.
