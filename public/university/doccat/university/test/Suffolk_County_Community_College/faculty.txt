<p>Founded in 1959, Suffolk County Community College has three main campuses in Selden, Brentwood and Riverhead.
It also boasts a low student/faculty ratio of 18:1, a highly respected  Honors program, championship athletic teams and devoted faculty.
The program also features a rapid prototyping machine.</p>
<p>All three campuses have an active Honors Program for students who have demonstrated academic excellence.
Classes never exceed 20 students and are often run with less.</p>
