<p>Many students transfer to four-year colleges and universities to complete their baccalaureate degree after attending SCCC.
It is the largest, first, and main campus of Suffolk County Community College.
The Grant Campus is also home to the Mechatronics Program, a machinist and fabrication job-training program funded by local industry and the largest federal grant ever received by the college.
<p>The nursing program is one of the largest two year, RN program in the country.
