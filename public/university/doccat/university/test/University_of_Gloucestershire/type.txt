<p>Oxstalls campus is located in the heart of Gloucester, only five minutes drive from the city centre.
This Faculty brings together a range of programmes and activities that embrace what is often described as the Government's 'modernisation agenda' of public services.
At the heart of this agenda is an inter-professional approach to policies in sport and physical activity, health, social work, social care, community regeneration, education and social exclusion.
