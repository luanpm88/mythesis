<p> The <strong>Royal Melbourne Institute of Technology</strong>, or &quot;<strong>RMIT</strong>&quot;, is an <a>Australia</a>n <a>public university</a> located in <a>Melbourne</a>, <a>Victoria</a>.
<p>According to the <a>THES - QS World University Rankings</a> in 2009, RMIT is ranked in the top 10 universities in Australia.
It is included in the top 100 universities in the World for producing work-ready graduates, and is one of 20 <a>institutes of technology</a> that feature in the listing.
Most schools also maintain their own field-relevant research collections, which are in addition to the <a>RMIT University Library</a> network (<em>see: <a>Notable school collections</a></em>).
