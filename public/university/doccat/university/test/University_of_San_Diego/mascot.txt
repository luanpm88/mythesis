The new Student Life Pavilion (SLP) opened in 2009 and hosts the university's new student dining area(s), offices for student organizations and event spaces.
USD athletes and teams are known as the <strong><a>Toreros</a></strong>, which is Spanish for &quot;<a>Bullfighter</a>s&quot;.
<p>The Toreros' lone national champion is Zuzana Lesenarova, who won the women's tennis singles championship in 2000 by defeating Stanford's Marissa Irvin 4-6, 6-3, 7-6.</p>
