<p><strong>McDaniel College</strong> is a private four-year <a>liberal arts college</a> in <a>Westminster, Maryland</a>, located 30 miles (50&nbsp;km) northwest of <a>Baltimore</a>.
McDaniel is also a member of the <a>Phi Beta Kappa</a> Honor society to which only 4.8% of <a>United States</a> colleges or universities belong.</p>
