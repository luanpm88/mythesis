According to RNCM figures, as of 2007 the student population is 696, with 496 undergraduates and 200 postgraduates; 49% male, 51% female.
<p>In January 2005, the RNCM was awarded &#x00a3;4.5 million by the <a>Higher Education Funding Council for England</a> to become a recognised Centre for Excellence in Teaching and Learning (CETL), the only UK conservatoire to be selected.
