<p><strong>Middlebury College</strong> is a private <a>liberal arts college</a> located in <a>Middlebury</a>, <a>Vermont</a>, <a>USA</a>.
The Winter Term, often called &quot;J-Term&quot;, allows students to enroll in one intensive course, pursue independent research, or complete an off-campus internship.</p>
The Institute currently enrolls 790 graduate students in the fields of <a>international relations</a>, <a>international business</a>, <a>language teaching</a>, and <a>translation</a> and <a>interpretation</a>.</p>
It is also one of the few schools with a bilingual requirement upon enrollment for all students.
<p>Six of Middlebury's summer schools&nbsp;&#x2014;  Chinese, French, German, Italian, Russian, and Spanish&nbsp;&#x2014;  offer graduate programs in addition to the undergraduate component.
<p>The college operates schools abroad at 34 locations, including: Argentina (<a>Buenos Aires</a> and <a>Tucum&#x00e1;n</a>); Brazil (<a>Belo Horizonte</a>, <a>Florian&#x00f3;polis</a>, and <a>Niteroi</a>); Chile (<a>Concepci&#x00f3;n</a>, <a>La Serena</a>, <a>Santiago</a>, <a>Temuco</a>, <a>Valdivia</a>, and <a>Valpara&#x00ed;so</a>); China (<a>Beijing</a>, <a>Hangzhou</a>, and <a>Kunming</a>); Egypt (<a>Alexandria</a>); France (<a>Paris</a>, <a>Poitiers</a>, and <a>Bordeaux</a>); Germany (<a>Berlin</a> and <a>Mainz</a>); Italy (<a>Ferrara</a> and <a>Florence</a>); Japan (<a>Tokyo</a>); Mexico (<a>Guadalajara</a> and <a>Xalapa</a>); Russia (<a>Irkutsk</a>, <a>Moscow</a>, and <a>Yaroslavl</a>); Spain (<a>Cordoba</a>, <a>Getafe</a>, <a>Logro&#x00f1;o</a>, and <a>Madrid</a>); and Uruguay (<a>Montevideo</a>).[<a>http://www.middlebury.edu/sa</a>]</p>
<p><em><a>U.S. News and World Report</a></em> ranks Middlebury as the 4th-best liberal arts college in the U.S., tied with <a>Wellesley</a>.
<p>According to the <em><a>Wall Street Journal</a></em>, Middlebury is one of the top &quot;feeder schools&quot; to elite graduate business, medical, and law schools.
<p>The 2011 <em><a>Newsweek</a></em> college rankings listed Middlebury as #23 of the &quot;<em>Most desirable schools in America</em>,&quot; #5 of the &quot;<em>Most desirable rural schools</em>,&quot; #9 of the &quot;<em>Most desirable small schools</em>,&quot; and #16 of the &quot;<em>Schools stocked with jocks</em>.&quot;</p>
<p><em><a>Forbes Magazine</a></em>, in its 2010 rankings of all universities and colleges in the United States, ranked Middlebury at #26.[<a>http://www.forbes.com/lists/2010/94/best-colleges-10_Americas-Best-Colleges_Rank_2.html</a>]</p>
<p>There are 19 works in Middlebury's campus public art collection, including <em><a>Frisbee</a></em> and a version of <a>Robert Indiana</a>'s <em><a>Love</a></em> series.
