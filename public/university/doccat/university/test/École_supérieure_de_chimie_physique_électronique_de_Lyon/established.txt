<p>It is located in <a>Villeurbanne</a>, near <a>Lyon</a>.</p>
<p>; Chemistry and chemical engineering : conception, synthesis and manufacture of new <a>molecule</a>s with high added value, with the necessity of being &#x201c;first on the market&#x201d;, and with concern for <a>the environment</a> and for <a>sustainability</a></p>
<p>; Electronics, telecommunications,maths, computer science : a global education in <a>electronics</a>, <a>microelectronics</a>, <a>computer science</a>, <a>image</a> and <a>signal processing</a>, and <a>telecommunications</a> and applied math</p>
<p>CPE Lyon is also well-known for its research in <a>organic chemistry</a>, analytical sciences, <a>chemical</a> and process engineering, <a>microelectronics</a>, and <a>image processing</a>.</p>
<h2>History</h2>
<li>1883 : The <em>&#x00c9;cole Sup&#x00e9;rieure de Chimie Industrielle de Lyon (ESCIL)</em> is founded by <a>Jules Raulin</a>, student of <a>Louis Pasteur</a></li>
<li>1919 : <a>Victor Grignard</a> awarded in 1912 with the <a>Nobel Prize in Chemistry</a>, becomes director of the <em>&#x00c9;cole Sup&#x00e9;rieure de Chimie Industrielle de Lyon (ESCIL)</em></li>
<li>1933 : The <em>&#x00c9;cole Sup&#x00e9;rieure de Chimie Industrielle de Lyon (ESCIL)</em> is awarded with the <a>L&#x00e9;gion d'Honneur</a> by the Ministry of War, for services rendered to the nation </li>
<li>2005 : <a>Jean Dercourt</a>, Secretary of the <a>French Academy of Sciences</a>, is appointed President of the <em>&#x00c9;cole Sup&#x00e9;rieure de Chimie Physique &#x00c9;lectronique de Lyon (CPE-Lyon)</em></li>
<li><a>Yves Chauvin</a> (ESCIL 1954) : 2005 <a>Nobel Prize in Chemistry</a></li>
<li><a>Jean-Marie Basset</a> (ESCIL 1965) : Member of the <a>French Academy of Sciences</a> (Acad&#x00e9;mie des sciences), received in 1991 the award from <a>Max Planck Society</a></li>
<li><a>Emile Kuntz</a> (ESCIL 1965) : awarded in 2005 with the <em>Ch&#x00e9;reau Lavet Prize</em> - Prize of the <a>French Academy of Technologies</a></li>
<li><a>Jean Fr&#x00e9;chet</a> (ICPI-C 1966) : <a>Henry Rapoport</a> Chair of <a>Organic Chemistry</a> at the Department of Chemistry, <a>University of California, Berkeley</a></li>
<li><a>Laurent de la Clergerie</a> (ICPI-E 1994) : Founder and Chairman of the Management Board of <a>LDLC.com</a></li>
<li><a>Bruno Bonnell</a> (ESCIL 1981) : Founder of <a>Infogrames Entertainment, SA</a>, Chairman of the Board, Acting Chief Financial Officer and Chief Creative Officer of <a>Atari, Inc.</a>.</li>
<li><a>Marcel M&#x00e9;rieux</a> (ESCIL 1891) : Student of <a>Louis Pasteur</a> and founder of Institut M&#x00e9;rieux that became <a>Sanofi Aventis</a>.
Institut M&#x00e9;rieux is a parent company of <a>bioM&#x00e9;rieux</a>.
