<p><strong>Collin College</strong>, formally known as the Collin County Community College District, is a <a>community college district</a> which serves <a>Collin</a> and <a>Rockwall</a> counties, located north and northeast of <a>Dallas</a>.
Founded in 1985, the district has grown as the county has grown: from around 5,000 students in 1986 to about 46,000 in 2009.
With the domain change to <em>collin.edu</em> in January 2010, the college finished rebranding itself and is now known as &quot;Collin College.&quot;
<p>As defined by the <a>Texas Legislature</a>, the official service area of Collin College is the following:</p>
<li>those portions of Denton County within the cities of <a>Frisco</a> and <a>The Colony</a>, and those portions of the county included within the <a>Celina</a> and <a>Prosper</a> school districts.</li>
