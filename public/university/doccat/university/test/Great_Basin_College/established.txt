Opened in 1967 as &quot;Elko Community College&quot;, it was later renamed to &quot;Northern Nevada Community College&quot; and then to its current name.
<p>The College offers two-year and four year degrees in a variety of disciplines.
It offers Associate of Applied Science degrees in the areas of business, computer office technology, criminal justice, early childhood education, industrial technology, and nursing.
Additionally, it offers four year degrees in elementary and secondary education, applied science, nursing and integrated professional studies.
Certificate programs and short-term training programs also exist in several business, secretarial/administrative assistant, and industrial arts fields.</p>
