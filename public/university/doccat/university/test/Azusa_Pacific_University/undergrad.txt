With nearly 5,000 students, APU's undergraduate student body is the largest in the <a>Council for Christian Colleges and Universities</a> (although <a>Indiana Wesleyan University</a> has a larger total student body) and the second largest evangelical undergraduate student body in the <a>United States</a>, behind only <a>Liberty University</a>.</p>
The university now offers more than 60 areas of undergraduate study, 26 master&#x2019;s degree programs, and 7 doctorates to a total student population of more than 8,100.</p>
More than 70 countries and all fifty states are represented in the student population.</p>
