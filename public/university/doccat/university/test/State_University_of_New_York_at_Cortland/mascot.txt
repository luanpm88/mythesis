<p>The <strong>Cortland Red Dragons</strong> are the athletic teams for SUNY Cortland.
The standings are based on schools' national finishes in different sports.</p>
The Cortland women captured seven NCAA Division III national championships in a nine-year span between 1989 and 1997 (1989, 1991&#x2013;95, 1997).
<p>In all, Cortland teams have won 23 national titles, including 17 NCAA crowns.
