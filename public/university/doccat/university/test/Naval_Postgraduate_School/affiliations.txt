<p>The NPS student population is mostly active-duty officers from all branches of the U.S. Military, although U.S. Government civilians and members of foreign militaries can also matriculate under a variety of programs.
<p>The <a>Air Force Institute of Technology</a> (AFIT) serves a similar purpose for the U.S. Air Force.
The U.S. Army does not have a comparable school; Army officers study at the <strong>Naval Postgraduate School</strong> or AFIT.</p>
