<p><strong>Fujian Agriculture and Forestry University</strong> (&#x798f;&#x5efa;&#x519c;&#x6797;&#x5927;&#x5b66;) is a public university in Jinshan, <a>Fuzhou</a>, <a>Fujian</a>, <a>China</a>.
<p>Fujian Agriculture and Forestry University's library collection contains more than 1.63 million volumes.</p>
<h2>History</h2>
<p>The university was founded in 1936.
It is now known as the Fujian Agriculture and Forestry University is public university focused on the development of agriculture and forestry.</p>
<p>Apart from its major campuses, this university owns and operates an independent college named Oriental College.Oriental College located in <a>Mawei</a>, Fuzhou.</p>
