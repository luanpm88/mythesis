<p>The Niagara University Community Action Program (NUCAP) and Learn &amp; Serve Niagara are the lead offices for the university's renowned community service program.
Generations of Niagara students have served as volunteers for area nursing homes, hospitals, and schools, the American Red Cross, the American Heart Association, and the Upper Mountain Volunteer Fire Company, among others.</p>
<li>In the field of law, Niagara alumni include: Jerome C. Gorski, Judge on the New York Supreme Court, Appellate Division; <a>Frank D. O'Connor</a>, former Judge on the New York Supreme Court, Appellate Division; <a>Hugh B. Scott</a>, Magistrate Judge of the United States District Court for the Western District of New York and the first African American to become an Assistant United States Attorney; and <a>Frederick J. Scullin</a>, Senior Judge on the United States District Court for the Northern District of New York.</li>
