<p>The  hilltop campus core is home to the nation's first <a>Charter</a> College of Education, a <a>NASA</a>-funded SPACE program, <a>Rockefeller</a>-supported humanities center, a <a>National Science Foundation</a> funded environmental research center and other award-winning engineering programs.
Notable LACHSA alumni include singer <a>Josh Groban</a>, actress <a>Jenna Elfman</a>, actor/singer <a>Corbin Bleu</a>, and UCLA Athletics senior executive Ron &quot;Country Club&quot; Kobata.</p>
<p>CSU Los Angeles is one of only eight institutions in North and South America selected as a Rockefeller Foundation humanities fellowship residency site.
