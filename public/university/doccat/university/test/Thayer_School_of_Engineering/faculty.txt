Over 350&nbsp;students are currently enrolled at Thayer, overseen by a faculty of 45 and preceded by over 4,000 living alumni of the school.</p>
During the late 19th century and early 20th century, the school's enrollment, funding, and faculty size steadily increased.</p>
As a College academic department, the school's undergraduate offerings are open to any Dartmouth student, including non-majors.</p>
<p> The Thayer School currently claims 74 active instructors, including 29 tenured or tenure-track faculty, 16 research or instructional faculty, 22&nbsp;adjunct faculty members, and seven lecturers.
