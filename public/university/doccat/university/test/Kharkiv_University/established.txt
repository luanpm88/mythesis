<p><strong> Kharkiv National University</strong> () in the city of <a>Kharkiv</a>, is one of the major universities in <a>Ukraine</a>, and earlier in the <a>Russian Empire</a> and <a>Soviet Union</a>.
It was founded in 1804 through the efforts of <a>Vasyl Karazin</a> (after whom the university is now named) becoming the second oldest university in Ukraine after the <a>University of Lviv</a>.</p>
