UWF intercollegiate athletics compete in the <a>NCAA</a> <a>Division II</a> <a>Gulf South Conference</a>.</p>
<p>Men's intercollegiate sports: Baseball, Soccer, Basketball, Tennis, Cross country, and Golf.</p>
<p>Women's intercollegiate sports: Basketball, Tennis, Cross country, Golf, Softball, Soccer, and Volleyball.</p>
