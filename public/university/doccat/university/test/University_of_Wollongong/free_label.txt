Wollongong UniCentre, an on-campus organisation and controlled entity of the University, provides the social and commercial infrastructure on the campus, administering the UniBar, student clubs and interest groups, food outlets, entertainment and activities, a books and news shop and other student services.</p>
<p><a>Campus News</a> is the University's official quarterly publication.</p>
<p>The magazine features student investigative and feature articles, news, artwork, opinion, film and music reviews, as well as interviews and editorials.
