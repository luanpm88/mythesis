<p>In October 2005 Professor Caroline Gipps became Vice-Chancellor - the University's first female Vice-Chancellor.</p>
Executive is led by the Vice-Chancellor assisted by two Deputy Vice-Chancellors and four Pro Vice-Chancellors.
These posts are elected annually by a cross-campus ballot.
These are co-ordinated by Active Volunteers, the University's volunteering agency.
