<p><strong>Calvin College</strong> is a comprehensive <a>liberal arts</a> <a>college</a> located in <a>Grand Rapids</a>, <a>Michigan</a>.
<p>Calvin College is accredited by the <a>Higher Learning Commission</a> of the <a>North Central Association of Colleges and Secondary Schools</a> Calvin offers majors or minors in 115 academic or pre-professional fields.
Core studies courses are designed to introduce students to a variety of disciplines, providing them with a greater understanding of the world and integration of ideas essential to a well-rounded liberal arts education.
Following the ideal of an integrated community, all of the buildings are intended serve a variety of purposes.
The ground floor features classrooms, a public atrium, and a snack cart.</p>
