Unlike many college campuses which feature an impressive structure at the center of the campus, such as a chapel or administrative hall, Calvin has no such building.
<p>The Fine Arts Center is one of the most quickly recognized buildings on campus, given its giant heptagon shape.
With many rare items, books, manuscripts, articles and literature, the Meeter Center is acclaimed as one of the most extensive  and user-friendly of all Calvin and Calvinism collections.
<p>Hekman Library is currently the second largest private academic library in the state.</p>
