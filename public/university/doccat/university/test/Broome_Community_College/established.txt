The college was founded in 1946 and went through several name changes.
<p><strong>BCC</strong> serves students from a single campus on Upper Front Street in <a>Binghamton, New York</a>, though some classes are taught in <a>Owego, New York</a> at a small classroom center.
<p>It offers a variety of classes to all students that plan on finishing their 2 year degree at <strong>BCC</strong> or hope to transfer to a 4 year school.
Most Broome Community College students transfer to Binghamton University after their first 2 years.</p>
<p>The Broome Community College Hornets participate in the NJCAA as a member of Region III.
