<h2>President</h2>
McGovern chose <a>Thomas Eagleton</a>.</p>
<p>President <a>Jimmy Carter</a> appointed him to a blue-ribbon immigration reform commission in 1979; the commission's finding &mdash; that any national immigration reform proposals can succeed only if the American national border is properly secured beforehand &mdash; has been cited by various opponents of <a>illegal immigration to the United States</a>, especially those that are Catholic or sympathetic to Catholic views.</p>
