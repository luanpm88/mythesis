<h2>Student life</h2>
Students who complete a prescribed course of study at MEC are granted preferred acceptance into four <a>SUNY Downstate Medical Center</a> degree programs: Physician Assistant Program, Physical Therapy Program, Diagnostic Medical Imaging Program, and Occupational Therapy Program.</p>
