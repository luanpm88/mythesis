<p>The <strong>Villahermosa Institute of Technology</strong> (in <a>Spanish</a>: <strong><em>Instituto Tecnol&#x00f3;gico de Villahermosa, ITVH</em></strong>) is <a>Mexican</a> <a>public university</a> located in <a>Villahermosa</a>, <a>Tabasco</a>, in the <a>Gulf of Mexico</a>.
<h2>History</h2>
<p>The Institute was founded on September 12, 1974.&lt;ref
name=&quot;itvh_history&quot; /&gt; On November 20, 1979 it moved to its current location at the Villahermosa Industrial Park.&lt;ref
<p>The Institute offers:</p>
<li><a>Bachelor's degree</a>s in Information Technology, Management, Computer Systems Engineering, Chemical Engineering, Biochemical Engineering, Industrial Engineering and Civil Engineering.
<li>Postgraduate diplomas in Information Technology and Food Quality.</li>
