established$$$@@@%%%From 1971 until 1998, <strong>Regents College</strong> operated as a program of the Board of Regents (which also served as its board of trustees) and under the authority of <strong><em>The University of the State of New York</em></strong> by which degrees and diplomas were awarded during that period.
established$$$@@@%%%In April 1998, the Board of Regents granted the College an absolute charter to operate as a private, nonprofit, independent institution.
