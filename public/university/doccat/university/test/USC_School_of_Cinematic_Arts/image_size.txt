<li>Since 1973, at least one alumnus of SCA has been nominated for an <a>Academy Award</a> annually, totaling 256 nominations and 78 wins.
<li>Since 1973, at least one SCA alumnus or alumna has been nominated for the <a>Emmy Award</a> annually, totalling 473 nominations and 119 wins.</li>
<li>In 1973, <a>Robert Zemeckis</a> won a Special Jury Award at the Academy of Motion Picture Arts &amp; Sciences' second annual Student Film Awards presentation for <em>A Field of Honor</em>.</li>
