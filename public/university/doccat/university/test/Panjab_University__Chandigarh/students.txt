The university has 58 teaching and research departments and 10 centres/chairs for teaching and research at the main campus located at Chandigarh.
<p>The centre has helped the states of Haryana, Panjab and Himachal Pradesh in solving problems associated with energy planning, management and environmental protection.
<p>There are fifteen hostels on the campus including seven hostels for about 2,000 men and five women hostels.
