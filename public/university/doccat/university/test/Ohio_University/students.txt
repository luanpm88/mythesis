<h3>School of Recreation and Sports Sciences</h3>
<p>The School of Recreation and Sport Sciences offers an array of academic programs at the graduate and undergraduate levels.
In addition, Ohio has played in the <a>NCAA</a> <a>Tournament</a> 12 times (<em>second most in the MAC</em>), appearing in 1960, 1961, 1964, 1965, 1970, 1972, 1974, 1983, 1985, 1994, 2005, and 2010.
<p>Ohio's <a>Olympic Sports</a> programs are also among some of the best in the MAC.
<p><a>Club Sports</a> are athletic organizations offered through universities that are available to the public body of students and faculty.
