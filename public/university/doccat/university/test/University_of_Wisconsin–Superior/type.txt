<p> <a>240px|thumb|right|Yellowjacket Union</a> The <strong>University of Wisconsin&#x2013;Superior</strong> (also known as <strong>UW&#x2013;Superior</strong>, <strong>UWS</strong> or <strong>Superior</strong>) is a public university located in <a>Superior, Wisconsin</a>.
<p>UW&#x2013;Superior has been designated as the public <a>liberal arts college</a> in the <a>University of Wisconsin System</a>, and is a member of the <a>Council of Public Liberal Arts Colleges</a>.
<li>Holden Fine Arts Center, named for campus benefactor Paul Holden: communicating arts, music, and visual arts programs, Wisconsin Public Radio studios (<a>KUWS</a>/WHSA), Manion Theatre</li>
