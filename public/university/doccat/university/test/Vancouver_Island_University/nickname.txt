The main campus is located in <a>Nanaimo</a>, and there are regional campuses in <a>Duncan</a> and <a>Powell River</a>, as well as a campus centre in <a>Parksville</a>.
In 1995 they were awarded the authority to offer degrees in their own right.
<p><a>right|thumb|250px|VIU Student Union Building</a> Comprising of 4 campuses many renovations and major developments have been made in the past few years to accommodate VIU's growing student and faculty body.</p>
Aboriginal elders are present on campus at VIU to provide social supports.
