<p>Several of Onondaga's athletic teams are consistently nationally ranked, and the 2006 and 2007 men's lacrosse team won the NJCAA national championship in May 2006 and 2007.
Onondaga is a member of the <a>Mid-State Athletic Conference</a>.Onondagas 2008-2009 Men's and women's lacrosse teams won the national championships for JUCO.
The mens lax (lacrosse) team won the NJCAA National Championship in 2010.</p>
