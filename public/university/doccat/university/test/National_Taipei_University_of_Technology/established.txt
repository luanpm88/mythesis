<p>The <strong>National Taipei University of Technology</strong> is located in the <a>Daan District</a> of <a>Taipei City</a>, <a>Taiwan</a>.
The university traces its origins to the establishment of the School of Industrial Instruction in 1912.
By the time the ROC government moved to Taiwan, this technical institution was renamed Provincial Taipei Institute of Technology and served as a junior <a>vocational technical college</a>.
In 1981, it was renamed <em>National Taipei Institute of Technology</em>, and in the 1990s upgraded its junior college status to that of a university, resulting in the final name change to <em>National Taipei University of Technology</em> (&#x570b;&#x7acb;&#x53f0;&#x5317;&#x79d1;&#x6280;&#x5927;&#x5b78;) in 1997.
<p>The University is divided into 6 Colleges each made up of various institutes and departments.</p>
