<p>University of Wisconsin&#x2013;Stout is made up of two parts: a main campus and north campus, which is made up of mostly residence halls.
All freshman and sophomores are required to live on campus for their first two academic years.
<p>UW&#x2013;Stout's sports teams are the Blue Devils, represented by a <a>blue flame</a>, and the school colors are navy blue and white.
