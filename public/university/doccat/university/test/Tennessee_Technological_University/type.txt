<p> <strong>Tennessee Technological University</strong>, popularly known as <strong>Tennessee Tech</strong>, is an accredited <a>public university</a> located in <a>Cookeville, Tennessee</a>, <a>US</a>, a city approximately seventy miles (110&nbsp;km) east of <a>Nashville</a>.
<p>Tennessee Tech is ranked among the Top 8 Public Schools in the <a>South</a> in <a>U.S. News &amp; World Report</a>'s 2007 edition of &quot;America's Best Colleges.&quot;
It was also ranked among the Top Public Schools in the South in the 2002, 2003, 2005 and 2006 college guides.
