<h2>Athletics</h2>
<p>Twenty-four sports programs, for both men and women, participate in <a>NCAA Division III</a>.
<h3>The Center for Athletics, Recreation and Fitness</h3>
<p>The College broke ground on the new $25 million athletic center, named the Center for Athletics, Recreation and Fitness, on May 30, 2008.
<p>The Center is expected to be fully completed by late fall/early winter, when the climbing wall is complete.
