<p><strong>Kazan State University</strong> is located in <a>Kazan</a>, <a>Tatarstan</a>, <a>Russia</a>.
This institution formed a big number of scientific areas and schools (mathematical, chemical, medical, linguistic, geological, geobotanical, etc.).
