<p>On May 12, 2010, Current Clarke President Joanne Burrows announced that effective August 1, 2010 the school would be renamed Clarke University</p>
The college consists of 15 buildings, an athletic field, and 3 parking lots.
Of Clarke's 16 buildings, 15 are located along Clarke Drive, making it a very walkable campus.
The theater has a capacity of approximately 600 people.
