<p><strong>Keimyung University</strong> (<strong>KMU</strong> or colloquially known as <em>Kei-dae</em>) is a private university in <a>South Korea</a>.
When completed, it will be one of the leading medical centers both in quality and physical scope in this country.</p>
<p>The Korean Language Institute of The Center for International Education at KMU offers international students a Korean language course.
