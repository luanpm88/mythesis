As of 2009, Metro State had the second largest enrollment of undergraduates of any college in Colorado.
In fall 2010, the college began offering master's programs in teacher education and accounting, with social work to begin in fall 2011.
Metro State has an enrollment of over 24,000 students.</p>
<p>The college mascot is the <a>Roadrunner</a>, which came into being because of the very nature of the campus.
