<p><strong>Loyola College</strong> is a <a>Jesuit</a> institution in <a>Chennai</a>, <a>India</a>.
The college admits both undergraduates and post-graduates and offers degrees in the <a>liberal arts</a>, <a>sciences</a> and <a>commerce</a>.
It is an autonomous institution affiliated with the <a>University of Madras</a>, which grants almost all degrees..</p>
<h2>History</h2>
<p><a>thumb|left|Loyola Church</a> Loyola College was founded in <a>1925</a> by a <a>French</a> Jesuit, Rev. Fr.
Francis Bertram, with the support of a group of <a>Jesuit</a>s who had had their grounding at the Universities of <a>Oxford</a> and <a>Cambridge</a>, and the <a>London School of Economics</a>.</p>
The Arts/Humanities stream includes English, Economics, History, Tamil and Sociology.
The college was ranked the number one college or institution for Science degrees in India by <em><a>India Today</a></em> magazine in <a>2003</a>, <a>2004</a>, <a>2005</a>, and <a>2006</a>.
In <a>2007</a> it achieved the unique distinction of being ranked first in both Science and Humanities.
<p>The college follows a credit-based semester pattern, and was among the first in the country to adopt the system.
Undergraduate students have to pass all examinations and obtain at least 90 credits in three years to be eligible for award of a degree.
<p>The college is located on a sprawling  campus in the heart of the Chennai, in <a>Nungambakkam</a>.
Its tree-lined pathways, academic buildings, steepled <a>Gothic</a> chapel and playgrounds make for a well-known landmark in the southern metropolis.
<p>The college has been conferred a &quot;College with Potential For Excellence&quot; status by the <a>University Grants Commission</a>.
It was also given a <img>10 Million grant (1 Crore) in 2006 by the UGC for continual improvement of facilities.Being one of the top ten colleges in the country, Loyola college is the most preferred destination for many world Universities to collaborate and build tie-ups.
