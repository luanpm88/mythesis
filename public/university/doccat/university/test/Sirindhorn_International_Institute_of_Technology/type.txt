It is located in <a>Pathum Thani</a>, <a>Thailand</a>.
The institute is part of the <a>LAOTSE</a> network, an international cooperation of leading universities in <a>Europe</a> and <a>Asia</a>.</p>
<p>For its graduate levels, the institute attracts a lot of bright graduates, as the institute gains supports from many national and international bodies (and use that as scholarships for graduate students).
<p>A number of <a>undergraduate</a> and research-based <a>graduate</a> programs are offered from five schools and one department.
<p>The institute enjoys a strong link with many universities, remarkably with its neighbor <a>Asian Institute of Technology</a> (AIT) and a group of Japanese universities and research centers, as well as a network of European universities such as <a>DAAD</a> <a>International Quality Network</a> (IQN) program and <a>LAOTSE</a> network.
