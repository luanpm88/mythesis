The University of Coimbra has approximately 20,000 students, and hosts one of the largest communities of <a>international student</a>s in Portugal, being the most cosmopolitan Portuguese university.</p>
<p> Students are represented by the students' union <a>Associa&#x00e7;&#x00e3;o Acad&#x00e9;mica de Coimbra</a> (AAC).
