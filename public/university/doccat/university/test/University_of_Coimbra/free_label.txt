<p>The university harbours a huge central library (<a>University of Coimbra General Library</a>), botanical gardens (<a>Botanical Garden of the University of Coimbra</a>), stadiums and other sports facilities (<em><a>Est&#x00e1;dio Universit&#x00e1;rio de Coimbra</a></em> sports complex and <em>Campo de Santa Cruz</em> stadium), an astronomical observatory, a publishing house, a private <a>chapel</a> (<a>S&#x00e3;o Miguel Chapel</a>), a theatre (Teatro Acad&#x00e9;mico de Gil Vicente), and many support facilities such as dining halls and studying rooms.
The AAC develops activities such as theatre, cinema, radio and television broadcast, music, choral singing, journalism or philately, as well as rowing, athletics and many other sports.
