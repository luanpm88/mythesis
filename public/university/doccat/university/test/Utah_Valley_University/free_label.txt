<h2>Athletics</h2>
<p>The athletic program is in <a>NCAA Division I</a>.
After numerous years of playing as an independent team, the Wolverines are now affiliated with the <a>Great West Conference</a>, which began its inaugural all-sports season in 2008-09.</p>
<p>The school has an independent, student-run weekly newspaper called the <a>UVU Review</a>.
