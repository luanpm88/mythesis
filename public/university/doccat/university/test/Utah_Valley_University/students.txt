In the most recent fall 2010 semester the school had 32,670 students attending.</p>
<p>When it was a community college, the school had approximately 8,000 students enrolled, and now has over 28,000 students, growing by approximately 3,000 students a year.
Currently there are 32,670 students enrolled for Fall semester 2010 at UVU.</p>
<p>About 88% of UVU students come from Utah but an increasing number of students are coming from different countries and states.
UVU's main campus encompasses  and includes 48 buildings.
The school's NCAA sports are men's and women's <a>basketball</a>, men's and women's <a>track and field</a>, men's and women's <a>cross country</a>, <a>baseball</a>, <a>wrestling</a>, <a>softball</a>, women's <a>soccer</a>, and women's <a>volleyball</a>.
