<h2>Athletics</h2>
<p>&lt;!-- Deleted image removed: <a>thumb|right|Athletics logo</a> --&gt; The Wilkes University Colonels compete in <a>NCAA Division III</a> athletics, and is a member of the <a>Middle Atlantic Conference</a> in all sports except wrestling, which is a member of the Metropolitan Wrestling Conference.
They made the playoffs and lost in the first round to the top seeded team, Desales.</p>
The Stark Learning Center (SLC), located on South River Street, is the largest building on campus.
