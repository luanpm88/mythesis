Founded by the <a>Society of Jesus</a> in 1877, it is one of 28 member institutions of the <a>Association of Jesuit Colleges and Universities</a>.
Based in <a>Denver, Colorado</a>, Regis University comprises three colleges: Regis College, The Rueckert-Hartman College for Health Professions and the College for Professional Studies.</p>
<p>Regis University was established by a group of exiled Italian Jesuits in 1877 in <a>Las Vegas, New Mexico</a>.
That institution was named Las Vegas College.</p>
<p>For the 14th consecutive year, Regis University was named a top school in the West at the Master's level by US News and World Report.
<p>Many students participate in service learning activities by volunteering with various organizations throughout the Denver area.
Sheeran, the goal of Regis University is to educate &quot;leaders in service to others.&quot;</p>
<p>Regis University played host to the rock icon, <a>Jimi Hendrix</a>, as well as the British rock band <a>Queen (band)</a>, that played their first concert in the United States (April 16, 1974).</p>
<p>On August 12, 1993, Pope John Paul II visited the Lowell campus of Regis University, where he met with President Bill Clinton for the first time.
The pope and the president landed in separate helicopters on Boettcher Commons amid tight security.

<li>Since 2000, the Lowell campus has been designated an <a>arboretum</a> with more than 500 trees and shrubs, including three championship trees.</li>
<p>Regis College houses the traditional, undergraduate (and Master's of Art in Education) programs.
<p>When Regis absorbed her sister school, <a>Loretto Heights College</a>, the Rueckert Hartman College for Health Professions was born.
The school is divided into three schools and two departments: Loretto Heights School of Nursing, School of Pharmacy, School of Physical Therapy, Department of Health Care Ethics, and Department of Health Services Administration.
The college offers three doctoral programs, Doctor of Nursing Practice (entirely on-line), Doctor of Physical Therapy, and Doctor of Pharmacy.</p>
In 2007, the School for Professional Studies was realigned into the College for Professional Studies, with the mission of providing higher education to working professionals.</p>
<p>Regis University is in the <a>Rocky Mountain Athletic Conference</a> along with Adams State, Chadron State, Colorado Christian,Colorado Mines, CSU-Pueblo, Fort Lewis, Mesa State, Metro State, New Mexico Highlands, Regis University, UC-Colorado Springs, U. of Nebraska-Kearney, Western New Mexico, Western State.

<h2>Institute on the Common Good</h2>

The Institute on the Common Good&#x2019;s Transformational Dialogue emerges from roots deep within Ignatius of Loyola&#x2019;s (and the Jesuit&#x2019;s) philosophy underlying communal discernment.
We continue to build on these roots using some of the latest research in the emerging field of dialogue.</p>
