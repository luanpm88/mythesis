<p>The institute has three campuses, the main campus is located on the <a>Kilkenny</a> Road just south of Carlow town, the second smaller campus is located off Summerhill Road and overlooking <a>Wexford</a> town and a third in the old Cleremont Convent in <a>Rathnew</a>, <a>County Wicklow</a>.
<p>Together there are approximately 4,000 students whose courses focus primarily on <a>business</a>, <a>science</a>, <a>technology</a>; these three disciplines are reflected in the institutes seal.
