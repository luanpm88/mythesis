<h2>History</h2>
<p>Lewis University was founded in 1932 by the <a>Chicago Archdiocese</a> and <a>Bishop</a> <a>Bernard J. Sheil</a> as the <em>Holy Name Technical School</em>.
The school gets its name from philanthropist Frank J. Lewis who funded the construction of many of the school's buildings.
In 1934 it was renamed the <em>Lewis Holy Name Technical School</em>.
The next year its name changed yet again to the Lewis Holy Name School of Aeronautics.
In 1940, the name of the school changed to the <em>Lewis School of Aeronautics</em>.
During <a>World War II</a>, normal classes were suspended as the campus was given to the <a>United States Navy</a> to train pilots.
Regular classes resumed in late 1944 and the college soon adopted a more traditional arts and science <a>curriculum</a>.
Three years later the school's name was changed to the <em>Lewis College of Science and Technology</em>.
The school's name was shortened to simply <em>Lewis College</em> in 1962 and finally received its current name of <em>Lewis University</em> in 1973.</p>
Lewis&#x2019; strengths as an institution of higher learning have been evidenced through various sources.
The University has been cited as one of the best colleges in the region for the last two consecutive years by both <a>The Princeton Review</a> and <a>U.S. News and World Report</a>.
The University is included in the <a>top tier</a> of <a>U.S. News and World Report</a>&#x2019;s rankings of the best Midwest master&#x2019;s-level universities, most recently placing 58 out of a total of 144 institutions.
<p>In 2010, Lewis University offers more than 80 undergraduate majors and programs of study, accelerated degree completion options for working adults, various aviation programs and 22 graduate programs in nine fields.

<p>Lewis University is a <a>NCAA</a> Division II school that is part of the <a>Great Lakes Valley Conference</a> and the <a>Midwestern Intercollegiate Volleyball Association</a>.</p>
<p>The men's volleyball team won the 2003 <a>NCAA Division I/II National Collegiate Men's Volleyball Championship</a> by defeating <a>Brigham Young University</a>, but it later voluntarily gave back its title after an internal investigation found ineligibility issues that were kept private from the NCAA.</p>
<p>Lewis also competes intercollegiately in Rugby as a member of the <a>Carfu</a>.</p>
