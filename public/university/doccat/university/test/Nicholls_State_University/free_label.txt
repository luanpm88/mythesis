<h2>Athletics</h2>
The Colonels advanced to the Division I-AA playoffs as the conference champion, based on their 32-29 overtime victory over then fifth-ranked Texas State, but eventually lost to the then No. 3 ranked <a>Furman</a> Paladins, 14-12, in Paladin Stadium in Greenville, S.C.</p>
<p>Nicholls official athletics mascot is the colonel.
President Stephen Hulbert stated that &quot;The Colonel is and will remain the mascot designation for Nicholls State University and its intercollegiate athletics program&quot; on March 31, 2004 while calling the current Colonel depiction antiquated and reminiscent of the Civil War in the South.
Hulbert banned the old Colonel from the university and tasked student leaders to work on a suitable replacement.
<p>Art Program Accreditation</p>
The Division of Art is accredited by the National Association of Schools of Art and Design.</p>
