<p><strong>Istanbul Technical University</strong> (<strong>ITU</strong>,  commonly referred to as ITU or Technical University) is an international <a>technical university</a> located in <a>Istanbul</a>, <a>Turkey</a>.
ITU is ranked 108th worldwide and 1st nationwide in the field of engineering/technology by <a>THES - QS World University Rankings</a> in 2009.
By promoting strong international relationships, this public university also offers its students truly universal educational opportunities which are not limited by national boundaries.</p>
For example, the Faculty of Electrical and Electronics Engineering consists of the departments of <a>electrical engineering</a>, <a>computer engineering</a>, <a>control engineering</a>, <a>electronics engineering</a> and <a>telecommunications engineering</a>.</p>

According to <a>THES - QS World University Rankings</a>, Istanbul Technical University is among the top 500 universities in the world and is the top university in Turkey in the field of technology and engineering.
<p>  ITU is a public (state) university.
<p>G&#x00fc;mu&#x015f;suyu (Mechanical Engineering Faculty) and Ma&#x00e7;ka (Management Faculty) campuses are close to <a>Taksim Square</a>, and they are also among the important historical buildings of Istanbul.</p>
<p><a>NHPCC</a>, is located at the Ayaza&#x011f;a Campus.
