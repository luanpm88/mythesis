<h2>Student life</h2>
<p>The academic buildings on campus include Egan Hall, Stafford Hall, <a>Saints Cosmas and Damian</a> Hall, and the <a>Saint Joseph</a> Center.</p>
<p><a>Saints Cosmas and Damian</a> Hall is the main science building.
<p>Starvaggi Hall is the main administrative building on campus.
<p>There are 12 residence buildings on campus: <a>Saint Francis</a> Hall, <a>Trinity</a> Hall, <a>Marian</a> Hall, <a>Saint Thomas More</a> Hall, <a>Saint Louis</a> Hall, <a>Saint Elizabeth</a> Hall, <a>Kolbe</a> Hall, <a>Clare</a> Hall, <a>Padua</a> Hall, <a>Saint Bonaventure</a> Hall, <a>Vianney</a> Hall, and <a>Scotus</a> Hall.
