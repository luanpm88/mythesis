<p>St Francis Xavier University is organized into the Faculty of Arts, Faculty of Science, <a>Gerald Schwartz School of Business</a> and Information Systems, School of Education, and the <a>Coady International Institute</a>.
All students in residence regardless of housing style are required to purchase a minimum amount of &quot;declining cash balance&quot; or DCB, which is loaded onto student ID cards and can be used at any of the food outlets on campus.
<p>X Alumnus, Eric Gillis (2003 CIS Cross Country Champion) competed in the <a>2008 Beijing Olympics</a> and placed 33rd in the 10 000m race.</p>
