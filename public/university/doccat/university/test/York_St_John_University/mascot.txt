<p>The Hockey club is one of the biggest clubs within the university offering 1st and 2nd mens and ladies teams and a mixed team.
The mens 1st team are the last ever BUSA National Plate champions, having won the competition in 2008, after which BUSA became BUCS and changed the national competition set-up.</p>
<p>Supporting the Sabbatical Officers in running the Union are Elected Officers that are elected each year in to certain portfollio's.
