<p>In recent years York St John has developed its research capacity in Allied Health Professions &amp; Studies; Psychology; Education; Sports-related Studies; English Language &amp; Literature; Theology &amp; Religious Studies; and Drama, Dance &amp; Performing Arts.
The scheme is administered by a former manager of Sheffield University Student Union.
<p>The Students' Union has many sports clubs.
The club has a large visible presence on the River Ouse and are often seen practicing early in the morning, starting at 7am, well before lectures.</p>
Each Officer is given a Sabbatical Mentor and they work together through out the year on different Projects.</p>
<p>The Fountains Learning Centre, opened in 2004, is located at the Clarence Street entrance to the campus.
