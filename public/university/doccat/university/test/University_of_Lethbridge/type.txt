<p>  The <strong>University of Lethbridge</strong> (also known as the <strong>U of L</strong>) is a public university in the liberal arts tradition, located in <a>Lethbridge</a>, <a>Alberta</a>, <a>Canada</a>, with two other urban campuses in <a>Calgary</a> and <a>Edmonton</a>.
<h2>Faculties and schools</h2>
<li>Faculty of Education</li>
<p>The Faculty of Arts and Science offers 9 pre-professional programs in dentistry, engineering, journalism, law, medicine, nutrition and food sciences, optometry, social work, and veterinary medicine.</p>
