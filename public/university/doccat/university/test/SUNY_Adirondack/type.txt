<p>Student Center houses the school bookstore, a <a>cafeteria</a>, a student lounge with big-screen TV, an <a>air hockey</a> table, and a number of couches, chairs, and tables for students to relax or sleep.
<p>Washington Hall is next to Eisenhart Hall and is home to nursing labs, the radio station (<a>WGFR</a>), the television studio (for instructional use), a video editing lab, photography labs and darkroom, and a <a>Mac</a> lab for graphic design/media arts students.</p>
The offices of most <a>English</a> and <a>Math</a> professors are located in the basement of this hall.
<a>Art</a>, <a>language arts</a>, and mathematics courses are primarily taught in this building.
