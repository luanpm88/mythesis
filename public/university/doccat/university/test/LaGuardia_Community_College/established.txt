<p><strong>LaGuardia Community College</strong> is a two-year <a>community college</a> located in the <a>Hunter's Point</a> section of <a>Long Island City</a> in the <a>New York City</a> borough of <a>Queens</a>, and is a component of the <a>City University of New York</a>.
LaGuardia is named after former Congressman and New York mayor <a>Fiorello H. LaGuardia</a>.
The college offers <a>associate degree</a>s in the arts, sciences, and <a>applied science</a>s, as well as <a>continuing education</a> programs.</p>
