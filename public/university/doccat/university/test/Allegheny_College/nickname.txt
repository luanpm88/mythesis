<li><strong>The Learning Commons</strong>, located in the Pelletier Library, has a staff to help students with research and study skills as well as disability services.

<p>Allegheny insists students choose a minor as well as a major A student's major can be in the humanities, social sciences or natural sciences, but that student's minor must be in a different division than his or her major.
