:<em>For other universities with a similar name, see <a>Southeast University (disambiguation)</a></em>.  <strong>Southeast University</strong> (SEU, <a>Pinyin</a>: D&#x014d;ngn&#x00e1;n D&#x00e0;xu&#x00e9;, <a>Simplified Chinese</a>: &#x4e1c;&#x5357;&#x5927;&#x5b66;), colloquially Dong Nan (<a>Pinyin</a>: D&#x014d;ng N&#x00e1;n , <a>Simplified Chinese</a>: &#x4e1c;&#x5357;) is a public, <a>coeducational</a> research university located in <a>Nanjing</a>, <a>Jiangsu</a> Province, <a>China</a>.
The SEU Architecture Design and Research Institute, which is one of the few national first-class design institutes, has undertaken various major projects.
