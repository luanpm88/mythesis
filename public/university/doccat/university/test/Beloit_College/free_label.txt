<p>Beloit students are offered a wide variety of housing options, ranging from specific substance-free dormitories to &quot;Special Interest&quot; houses, such as the Art, Spanish, Outdoor Environmental Club (OEC), Interfaith options, and, on a trial basis through 2012, gender-neutral housing.
A wide variety of student clubs bring visitors (musicians, artists, poets) to campus frequently.
<p>Beloit College has an Ultimate Frisbee club team BUFF (Beloit Ultimate Frisbee Family) and intramural teams during each spring semester.</p>
