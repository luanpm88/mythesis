<p> <strong>Beloit College</strong> is a private coeducational <a>liberal arts</a> <a>college</a> in <a>Beloit, Wisconsin</a>, <a>USA</a>.
The 1999 National Study of Student Engagement ranked Beloit in the top 20% of five benchmark categories measuring the quality of the student experience, one of just four schools to achieve this ranking.</pre>
A study abroad program to <a>Morocco</a> and <a>Estonia</a> is targeted at psychology majors (although any student may apply for the program), where they engage in cross-cultural studies.</p>
