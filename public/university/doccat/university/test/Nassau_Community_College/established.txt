<h2>History</h2>
<p><a>thumb|left|Buildings P and T</a> Created as part of the <a>State University of New York</a> (SUNY) in 1959.
When the college opened, on February 1, 1960, it had 632 students, and classes were held in an old courthouse.
When <a>Mitchel Air Force Base</a> was closed, the college obtained substantial property, including buildings to develop its new campus, on what is now known as <a>Mitchel Field</a>.
(The government still retains some housing and other facilities in the vicinity of the Nassau campus.)
