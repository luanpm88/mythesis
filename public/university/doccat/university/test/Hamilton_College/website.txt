<p>Generally, events sponsored by Greek-organizations occur on campus and are open to all students.</p>
<h3>On-campus events</h3>
<p><strong>Class and Charter Day</strong>: On the last day of spring term classes, all afternoon classes are cancelled for a campus wide picnic and party.
During the ceremony, chosen students are also inducted into Hamilton's three prestigious honor societies: Doers and Thinkers (13 rising sophomores), Was Los (6 rising juniors), and Pentagon (5 rising seniors).
