<p><strong>Louis Pasteur University</strong> (<em>Universit&#x00e9; Louis-Pasteur</em>), also known as <strong>Strasbourg I</strong> or <strong>ULP</strong> was a large university in <a>Strasbourg</a>, <a>Alsace</a>, <a>France</a>.
On 1 January 2009, Louis Pasteur University became part of the refounded <a>University of Strasbourg</a> and lost its status as an independent university.
<p>According to the <a>Academic Ranking of World Universities</a>, when it was an independent university, the ULP belonged to the first 100 rank (96th in 2006).</p>
<p>The university was a member of the <a>LERU</a> (League of European Research Universities).
It was named after the famous 19th-century French scientist <a>Louis Pasteur</a>.</p>
