Each student attending BSU pays a 43 cent per credit fee to sustain the <a>Minnesota State University Student Association</a>, a student-led non-profit that advocates on behalf of all Minnesota State University Students.</p>
<p>&lt;!-- Deleted image removed: <a>thumb|100px|right|Bucky, the Bemidji State University mascot.</a>
--&gt; The school sports teams are called the Beavers and compete in NCAA <a>Division II</a>, except in men's and women's <a>ice hockey</a>.
The Beavers are members of the <a>Northern Sun Intercollegiate Conference</a> in most sports, with both hockey teams competing at the <a>Division I</a> level.</p>
The men's ice hockey team qualified for the <a>2009 NCAA Division I Men's Ice Hockey Tournament</a>.
The team later defeated <a>Cornell</a> to advance to its first Division I <a>Frozen Four</a> appearance, but then lost to <a>Miami University</a> in the semifinals.</p>
