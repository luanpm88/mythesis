<p><strong>Seneca College of Applied Arts and Technology</strong> is a <a>community college</a> for applied arts and technology in <a>Toronto</a>, <a>Ontario</a>.
Seneca College is a member of <a>Polytechnics Canada</a>.</p>
<h2>History</h2>
<p>The college was established during the formation of Ontario&#x2019;s college system in 1967.
The school was founded as part of a provincial initiative to provide career-oriented <a>diploma</a> and <a>certificate</a> courses, as well as <a>continuing education</a> programs to <a>Ontario</a> communities.</p>
<p>The College is located in the <a>Greater Toronto Area</a> and has several campuses.

All in-flight training is undertaken through the facilities at the Buttonville Airport.
Classroom-based instruction is divided between Markham and Buttonville Campuses.</p>
