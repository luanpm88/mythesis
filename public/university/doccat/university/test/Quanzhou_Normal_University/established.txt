<h2>History</h2>
<p>The university was established in <a>1958</a>.
Shishan Campus is near the famous Gaogai Mount towards the south and Pengfeng to the north.
It is in <a>Nan'an</a> city.</p>
<p>Quanzhou Normal University has total enrollment of over 10,000 students and offers almost 100 programs of study in nine schools.
The library has contained more than one million books and reference materials and 800,000 specialised books.</p>
