<p><a>thumb|Higgins Hall center section rebuilt by Steven Holl</a> <a>thumb|DeKalb Avenue gate</a> <a>thumb|upright|Campus has many sycamores</a> Pratt Institute is an enclosed landscaped  campus in <a>Clinton Hill, Brooklyn</a> with historic buildings, library, and an athletic center.
Buildings on campus include the Library, Dekalb Hall, ISC Building, Main Building, North Hall, East Building, Student Union, Memorial Hall, Machinery Building, Chemistry Building, Engineering Building, Pratt Studios, Steuben Hall, and the ARC building.
