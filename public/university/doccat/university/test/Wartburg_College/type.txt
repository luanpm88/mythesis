<p><strong>Wartburg College</strong> is a selective four-year liberal arts college of the <a>Evangelical Lutheran Church in America</a> located in <a>Waverly</a>, <a>Iowa</a>.
Wartburg is the only private college in Iowa offering a <a>music therapy</a> major.
<a>Waverly</a> and <a>Eisenach</a> are sister cities, and they often swap foreign exchange students.
<p>The total cost of attendance at Wartburg is approximately $36,995 annually (2009&#x2013;10).
