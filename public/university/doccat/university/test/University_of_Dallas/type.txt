The curriculum for pre-med students has been crafted in such a way that a student may pursue a traditional liberal arts program as well as satisfy entrance requirements for medical school.
Courses offered include <a>Biology</a>, <a>Organic Chemistry</a>, <a>Biochemistry</a>, <a>Anatomy</a>, <a>Physics</a>, <a>Physiology</a>, <a>Microbiology</a>, <a>Immunology</a>, <a>Genetics</a>, <a>Microbiology</a>, <a>Molecular Biology</a>, <a>Biostatistics</a>, <a>Developmental biology</a>, and <a>Physical Chemistry</a>.</p>
