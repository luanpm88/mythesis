<p>Each major requires students to choose a concentration, or area of study.
<p>Students pursuing either the BABA or BAPA must take core courses in their area of study, including an experiential learning course and courses in their respective areas of concentration.</p>
Basic courses are designed to introduce the student to the concepts guiding the evolution of the Social Sciences and recent developments which are likely to determine future directions.
Students develop job skills that are needed to excel in the industry, such as critical thinking and communication skills.
