<h2>Student life</h2>
In most courses, students are assessed on the level of their class participation, a group project, a mid-term test, and possibly, an exam at the end of the term.
<p>It is part of each student's curriculum to spend at least 10 weeks on internship at an approved organisation, and 80 hours on community service.
Every matriculated SMU undergraduate is a member of the SMUSA.
<p>The elections for the Executive Committee of the Association are held during the second term of each academic year, and each member holds a one-year term of office, beginning from January 1 to December 31.</p>
