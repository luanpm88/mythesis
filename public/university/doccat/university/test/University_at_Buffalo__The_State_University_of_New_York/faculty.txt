<h3>Initial years</h3>
<p>The University at Buffalo has accumulated 27,700 undergraduate and <a>graduate students</a>, as well as 14,000 employees, across three campuses in the last 160 years.
Connecting all three campuses, as well as the facilities UB uses, is also a major element of the project.
<p> UB has nine libraries on its North (Amherst), South (Buffalo), and Downtown (Buffalo) campuses.
<p>Buffalo has three fight songs: &quot;Victory March&quot;, &quot;Go For a Touchdown&quot;, and &quot;Buffalo Fight Song&quot;.</p>
