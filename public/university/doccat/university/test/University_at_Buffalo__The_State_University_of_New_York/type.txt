<p><strong>State University of New York at Buffalo</strong>, commonly known as the <strong>University at Buffalo</strong> or <strong>UB</strong>, is a <a>public</a> research university which has multiple campuses located in <a>Buffalo</a> and <a>Amherst, New York</a>, USA.
In 2010, UB is ranked #51 by U.S. News and World Report in the ranking of top engineering schools.</li>
In 2010, UB is ranked #59 by U.S. News and World Report in the ranking of best medical schools.
It is consistently ranked among the top pharmacy schools in the United States.
However, as of November 2009, UB no longer ranked in the top 500 supercomputing sites in the world.</p>
Students also find housing in private locations.
