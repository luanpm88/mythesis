As of 2008 the medical school accepted around 100 graduates, 175 undergraduates and 10 students from overseas making it one of the larger UK medical schools.
Following <a>Quality Assurance Agency</a> inspections St George's gained a score of 23 out of 24 for its teaching of medicine  &#x2014; the highest of any of the London medical schools and, by academic teaching standards, the best medical school in London, second only to <a>Oxford</a> and <a>Cambridge</a> among England's 25 or so medical schools.
