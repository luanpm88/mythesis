<p>The <strong>University of Puerto Rico at Ponce</strong> (<strong>UPRP</strong> or <strong>UPR-Ponce</strong>) is a <a>state university</a> located in the municipality of <a>Ponce</a>, <a>Puerto Rico</a>.
UPRP is the only <a>campus</a> of the <a>University of Puerto Rico System</a> in the southern region of the island.
It is also known as <strong>CUTP</strong> from its former name of <em>Colegio Universitario Tecnol&#x00f3;gico de Ponce</em> in <a>Spanish</a>.</p>
<p>The campus, with about 3.265 students, is very close to many tourist attractions the city of Ponce offers.
The school offers associate and bachelor degrees in the following areas:</p>
<li>Associate Degree in Industrial and Physical Therapy</li>
<li>Bachelor of Natural Science with concentration in Biology or Biomedical with areas of emphasis in Biodiversity Assessment, Biotechnology and Marine Coastal Environment Assessment.</li>
<p>The UPRP has programs that combine study and work concurrently (practice) in the following areas: <a>Physical Therapy</a>, Athletic Therapy, Office Systems, <a>Pedagogy</a>, <a>Forensic Psychology</a>, <a>Psychology</a> and Mental Health, <a>Accounting</a>, <a>Finance</a> and <a>Information Systems</a>.</p>
<p>The University of Puerto Rico at Ponce also serves as a starting point for students interested in applying to engineering programs at the <a>Mayag&#x00fc;ez Campus</a> through what is known as articulated transfer.</p>
