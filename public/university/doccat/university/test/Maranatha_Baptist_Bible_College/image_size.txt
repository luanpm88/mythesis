<h2>Student life</h2>
Carey and Melford halls are assigned to the State Street Ministry, an evangelistic effort aimed at <a>University of Wisconsin&#x2013;Madison</a> students.
Judson and Weeks halls visit with elderly residents at Beverly Terrace Nursing Home, and tutor homeless and at-risk children at the Milwaukee Rescue Mission.
