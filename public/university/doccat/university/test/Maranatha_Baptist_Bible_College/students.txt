<p>Maranatha has five men's and five women's dormitories; Spurgeon, Judson, Leland, Armitage, and Carey men's halls are on the south side of campus, while Day, Weeks, Melford, Hilsen, and Gould women's halls are in the northwest corner of the campus.</p>
<p>Students can voluntarily participate in one of five ministry societies that are engaged in <a>evangelism</a> and social service.
