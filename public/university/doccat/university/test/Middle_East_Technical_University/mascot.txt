The university puts special emphasis on research and education in <a>engineering</a> and <a>natural sciences</a>, offering about 40 undergraduate programs within 5 faculties, and 97 masters and 62 doctorate programs within 5 graduate schools.
Campus area is 660,000 m&#x00b2;, close to the shore and surrounded by lemon trees.
