<p>As of 2010, METU has 19 international <a>joint degree programmes</a> with European and American universities at the undergraduate and graduate levels.
<p>As of 2009, the METU-Technopolis project employs about 3,300 personnel, approximately 2,700 of which are researchers (86% of the total staff are university graduates, and 23% have MS, MA, or PhD degrees), working within 240 firms.
The event with the largest number of participants is the annual METU Spring Festival, a five day long series of open air concerts and exhibitions held at the main campus.</p>
