Duquesne also hosts a Student Government Association, a student-run Program Council, a Commuter Council, a representative Residence Hall Association, an Interfraternity Council, Panhellenic Council, the <a>Knights of Columbus</a>, and numerous departmental Honor Societies.
<p>Duquesne is the home of the <a>Tamburitzans</a>, the longest-running multicultural song and dance company in the United States.
Furthermore, Duquesne's Center for Environmental Research and Education (CERE) offers undergraduate and graduate degrees in environmental science and management.</p>
