<p>Since 2001, Duquesne has offered an Italian campus program.
The faculty of the program, largely constituted by visiting professors and resident scholars, is supplemented by a few distinguished professors from the home campus.</p>
Duquesne has an ACHA Division I ice hockey program.</p>
Duquesne's new Power Center facility has also achieved a <a>LEED</a> Silver Rating.
Singer <a>Bobby Vinton</a>, MLB pitcher <a>Joe Beimel</a>, and big-band composer <a>Sammy Nestico</a> are also alumni.</p>
