<p><strong>Dalian Nationalities University</strong> () is located in <a>Dalian</a>, <a>Liaoning</a>, <a>China</a> and directly supervised by the State Ethnic Affairs Commission.</p>
<h2>History</h2>
<p>Dalian Nationalities University (Northeast Nationalities University before 1997) was founded in 1984.
<p>Students of DLNU have won numerous awards in various scientific and technological competitions, including winning Mathematical Contest in Modeling (MCM) 2005, representing China to compete in Robocup 2007, Winning Microsoft Imagine Cup in China 2008 and Second Price internationally .
<p>Dalian Nationalities University has a good reputation in both engineering and science programs.
The university also offers programs in business, economy, arts and management.
Fifteen state and provincial key laboratories have been established one after another throughout the years.</p>
