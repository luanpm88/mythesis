School colors are <a>black</a> and <a>magenta</a> and the school's mascot is the &#x201c;Fighting Muskie&#x201d; (the <a>muskellunge</a>, the largest member of the pike family).
<p>Graduate programs are also offered in education and management information systems, strategy and technology.
