<p>With school colors of purple and white, Mount Union competes in the <a>Ohio Athletic Conference</a> and in <a>NCAA</a> <a>Division III</a> athletics.
The school also sponsors 10 women's varsity teams: basketball, cross-country, golf, indoor track, outdoor track, soccer, <a>softball</a>, swimming, tennis and <a>volleyball</a>.</p>
The Purple Raiders won 110 consecutive regular-season games between 1994 and 2005, posted 14 undefeated regular seasons, won 16 Ohio Athletic Conference Championships, and had the best overall record in the 1990s (120-7-1 .941).</p>

Every spring semester alternates between a musical and a straight play every year for the main stage show, and every spring there are also student-directed one-acts.
Theatre productions are open to all students, regardless of major or class rank.</p>
