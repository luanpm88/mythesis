<p><strong>Lamar University</strong>, often referred to as <strong>LU</strong>, is a comprehensive <a>coeducational</a> <a>public</a> <a>university</a> located in <a>Beaumont</a>, <a>Texas</a>.
<p>In 1975, the university merged with Port Arthur College in <a>Port Arthur, Texas</a>, creating Lamar University-Port Arthur.
