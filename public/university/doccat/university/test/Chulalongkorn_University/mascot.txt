The emblem is '<a>Phra Kiao</a>', or Thai style coronet which is made to fit hair topknot.
<p>Undergraduate students are required to wear uniform as they are studying, taking examinations and contacting the university.
The buttons are made of metal and Phra Kiao emblem must be pinned on right chest.
The dark (slightly light) blue or black pleated skirt and any black, white, brown shoes that conceal heel are also required.
<p>There are also special graduation gown made of fine white net formed into long sleeved gown.
