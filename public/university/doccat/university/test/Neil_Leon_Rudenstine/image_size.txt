<p> <strong>Neil Leon Rudenstine</strong> (born January 21, 1935) is an <a>American</a> educator, literary scholar, and administrator.
He is also a Fellow of the <a>American Academy of Arts and Sciences</a>, a former director of the <a>American Council on Education</a>, and a member of the <a>Council on Foreign Relations</a>, the <a>American Philosophical Society</a>, and the <a>Committee for Economic Development</a>.
