<p>The <strong>Royal Conservatory of The Hague</strong> (in Dutch: <a>Koninklijk</a> Conservatorium Den Haag) is a <a>conservatorium of music</a>, providing <a>higher education</a> in music and <a>dance</a>, it is located in <a>The Hague</a>, <a>Netherlands</a>.</p>
<h2>The Conservatory</h2>
<p>The Conservatory was founded by <a>King William I</a> in 1826. </p>
<h3>Courses</h3>
<p>The Royal Conservatory offers Bachelors and Masters courses in the following areas:</p>
<ul>
  <li>European <a>classical music</a></li>
  <li><a>Early Music</a></li>
  <li><a>Jazz</a></li>
  <li><a>Sonology</a></li>
  <li>Image and Sound/ArtScience</li>
  <li><a>Art of Sound</a></li>
  <li><a>Musical composition</a></li>
  <li><a>Ballet</a></li>
  <li><a>Opera</a></li>
</ul>
