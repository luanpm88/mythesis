<p>   <strong>Hebei Medical University</strong> () is a university in <a>Shijiazhuang</a>, <a>Hebei</a>, <a>China</a>,under the provincial government.... a capital city of Hebei Province which is 3 hours away from Beijing by car and 1 and a half hours by train ,  <strong>Hebei Medical University</strong> Established in 1894, it is one of the oldest and AAA graded medical schools in China.</p>
<h2>Staff</h2>
<p>The staff numbers are over 7,100. Of these, 366 are professors, senior researchers and chief physicians. 967 are associate professors and also have the Indian teacher's staff  for the students from India.</p>
<h2>Students</h2>
<p>There are 25,600 students.</p>
<p>Hebei Medical University has long history of enrolling international students from countries including the <a>USA</a>, <a>Japan</a>, <a>Korea</a>, <a>Philippines</a>, <a>Taiwan</a>, the <a>UK</a>, <a>India</a>, <a>Pakistan</a> and <a>Nepal</a>. Currently there are over 400 international students.</p>
<h2>Colleges</h2>
<p>There are 14 colleges, and 15 specialities.</p>
<h2>Affiliated hospitals</h2>
<p>The university has six affiliated hospitals, in which there are 4,566 in-patient beds. Four hospitals have been granted the status of &quot;first-class hospitals in the third category&quot;. In addition, it also has 60 teaching hospitals and practice bases providing students with sites of clinical teaching, specialist teaching and practice.</p>
<h2>Achievements</h2>
<p>It carried out the first finger replantation surgery in China, and made the first diagnosis in the world of acute motor axonal neuropathy.</p>
<p>It has 9 subjects and 4 laboratories assessed at provincial advanced level, and 6 research institutes at provincial level. The Chinese Academy of Preventive Medical Science has set up its Hebei Academic Branch in this university. Each year nearly 200 research projects are undertaken. In addition three national and eight provincial academic periodicals have been compiled and published here.</p>
<p>MBBS is taught in English as well as many other courses, something that others universities cannot do, such as Traditional Chinese Medicine, Acupuncture and Massage, Clinic Medicine, Clinic Medicine of Integration of Traditional Chinese Medicine and Western Medicine, Stomatology, Anesthesiology, Preventive Medicine, Medical Imaging, Advanced Nursing Science, Chinese Pharmacology, Pharmacology, Pharmaceutics.</p>
