<p><strong>Padmabhushan Vasantdada Patil Pratishthan&#x2019;s College of Engineering</strong> (<strong>PVPP COE</strong>) is an engineering college in <a>Mumbai</a>, <a>India</a> (established 1991). </p>
<p>Padmabhushan Vasantdada Patil Pratishthan&#x2019;s College of Engineering is a tribute to the late Shri <a>Vasantdada Patil</a>, the Sahakar Maharshi and former Chief Minister of Maharashtra. The college is registered under Societies&#x2019; Registration Act, 1860 and Bombay Public Trust Act, 1950. Former <a>deputy Prime Minister</a> <a>Yeshwantrao Chavan</a>, laid the <a>foundation</a> of the college.</p>
<p>Established in 1991, the college is spread over a total area of . It is located in the centre of <a>Mumbai</a>, on the <a>Eastern Express highway</a> at <a>Sion</a>. It is approved by the <a>Government of Maharashtra</a>, <a>University of Mumbai</a>, and <a>All India Council for Technical Education</a> (AICTE) to conduct degree courses in Electronics, Electronics and Telecommunications, Computer Engineering, Information Technology Marine Engineering and diploma courses.</p>
