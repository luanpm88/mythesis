<p> <a>right|thumb|Hebut</a> <strong>Hebei University of Technology</strong> (<strong>HEBUT</strong>; ) is a public university whose main campus lies in the Guangrong Dao neighborhood of the <a>Hongqiao</a> District in <a>Tianjin</a>, <a>China</a>. It is the first <a>institute of technology</a> in modern Chinese education history. It is among the first group of institutions of higher learning in the national &#x201c;<a>211-Project</a>&#x201d; to which priority is given in construction. In order to carry out the &#x201c;21st Century Education Revitalizing Action Plan&#x201d;.</p>
<h2>History</h2>
<p>The institution was established by <a>Yuan Shikai</a>, receiving a royal charter in 1903 as <a>Peiyang</a> Technological School, from <a>Guangxu Emperor</a>. The university modeled itself on the famous European and American institutions of higher learning and aimed to rejuvenate China by training qualified personnel with new scientific and technological knowledge. In 1958, after restructuring, institution was renamed <a>Hebei institution of Technology</a>. In 1995, institution was renamed Hebei University of Technology.</p>

<h2>General information</h2>
<p>The university occupies an area of 3,400 Ares, and has a built-up area of 800,000 m&#x00b2;, including the two modern libraries,  collection of 1.6 million volumes.</p>
<p>Hebei University of Technology currently has working staff of over 2,100, including about professors &amp; associate professors, 16 academicians and more than 2 special engaged professors of &#x201c;Yangtze Scholar Award Project&#x201d; of the Ministry of Education.  The total student population is 20,500 with 3200 doctoral students and students for Master&#x2019;s degree.  Over 80,000 students have graduated from Hebei University of Technology.</p>
<p>For the time being, the university has 17 disciplines that confer Doctoral degree, 79 disciplines conferring Master&#x2019;s degree. The university also confers Master&#x2019;s degrees in business administration (MBA). At present there are 15 postdoctoral research programs.</p>
<p>The university has a new campus in Shuangkou Township, Beichen District.  The campus is partially completed and classes are being taught.</p>
