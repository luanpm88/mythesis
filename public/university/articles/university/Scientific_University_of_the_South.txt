<p>The <strong>Universidad Cient&#x00ed;fica del Sur</strong>, or <strong>UCSUR</strong>, is a higher education and research institution located in <a>Lima</a>, <a>Peru</a>.  It has a four-hectare campus close to the <a>Pantanos de Villa Reserved Zone</a>, in <a>Villa El Salvador</a>.</p>
<h2>History</h2>
<p>The Universidad Cient&#x00ed;fica del Sur was founded in 1996 by a Promoting Association led by Jos&#x00e9; Carlos Dextre Chac&#x00f3;n and an Organizing Commission led by Fernando Cabieses.</p>
<p>Two years later, the National Commission on the Functioning of Universities (CONAFU) approved the temporary activity of UCSUR by Resoluci&#x00f3;n N&#x00b0;356-98-CONAFU. Dr. <a>Fernando Cabieses</a> Molina was appointed as rector. </p>
<p>The first official ceremony took place the 26th of March, 1998, in Museo de Osma. The admission campaign started in July. </p>
<p>The first semester began with two schools (Medicine and Engineering) and fifty students. The Campus was originally in <a>Surco</a> district. However, a few years later, it was moved to its current location, close to the <a>Pantanos de Villa Reserved Zone</a>.</p>
<p>In March 2006, CONAFU awarded the <a>Definitive Authorization Resolution</a> to the university, which implies institutional autonomy. </p>
<p>Today, the university has over 1500 students in undergraduate and postgraduate courses.</p>

<h2>Organization</h2>
<h3>Administration</h3>
<p>A board is in charge of the university's administration. The current president of the board is Jos&#x00e9; Carlos Dextre Chac&#x00f3;n. Among several functions, the board is responsible of appointing the <a>Rector</a> and the <a>Dean</a> of faculty. Also, it coordinates with the Rector's office to take care of financial and academic issues.</p>

<h3>Research</h3>
<p>The university motivate the development of research project by the staff and the students. The research projects are administrated by the Vice-Rector's Office.</p>
<h3>CAMBIE Foundation</h3>
<p>CAMBIE Foundation has the mission of developing an environmental conscience in Peruvian society. Also, promotes and recognize projects that contributes to the conservation of natural resources.</p>
