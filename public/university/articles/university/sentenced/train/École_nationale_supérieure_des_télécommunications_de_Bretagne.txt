<p><strong>Telecom Bretagne</strong> (formerly known as ENST Bretagne) is a <a>French</a> research centre and high level training institution in Information Technologies and <a>telecommunications</a>.
This <em><a>grande &#x00e9;cole</a></em> of <a>engineering</a> is located near <a>Brest</a> in <a>Brittany</a>.</p>
<p>As a member of the <a>Institut Telecom</a>, it has three campuses:</p>
<ul>
  <li><a>Plouzan&#x00e9;</a>, in the <a>Technop&#x00f4;le Brest-Iroise</a>, near <a>Brest</a> (France);</li>
  <li><a>Campus de Beaulieu</a>, in <a>Rennes</a> (France);</li>
  <li><a>Supaero</a> campus, in <a>Toulouse</a> (France).</li>
</ul>
<p>Telecom Bretagne has been the source of breakthroughs in the world of <a>telecommunication</a>s, notably the <a>turbo code</a>s (first published in Proc.
IEEE <a>ICC</a>'93) used extensively in <a>3G</a> mobile telephony standards.</p>
<h2>History</h2>
<ul>
  <li><strong>1974:</strong> Pierre Lelong - Secretary of State for the <a>PTT</a> (literally <em><a>Postal Telegraph and Telephone</a></em>) - decides to establish a second school of <a>telecommunications</a> that will be located in the Brest area.</li>
<li><strong>1977:</strong> Creation of ENST Bretagne in Brest.
Admission of the first student body which counted 31 students.</li>
<li><strong>1986:</strong> Creation of the <a>Rennes</a> site.</li>
<li><strong>1997:</strong> Creation of the <a>Groupe des &#x00c9;coles des T&#x00e9;l&#x00e9;communications</a> (GET).</li>
<li><strong>2008:</strong> Renamed Telecom Bretagne.</li>
</ul>
<h2>Curriculum</h2>
<p><a>thumb|right|250px|Locations of GET schools</a> For students admitted in <em>formation initiale</em> (FI, literally <em>initial formation</em>), the curriculum takes 3 years and deals with six main domains in 1&lt;sup&gt;st&lt;/sup&gt; and 2&lt;sup&gt;nd&lt;/sup&gt; year:</p>
<ul>
  <li><a>Mathematics</a> and <a>signal processing</a></li>
  <li><a>Electronics</a> and <a>physics</a></li>
  <li><a>Computer science</a></li>
  <li><a>Networks</a></li>
  <li><a>Economy</a> and <a>social sciences</a></li>
  <li>Languages and intercultural dimensions</li>
</ul>
<p>and 4 options in 3&lt;sup&gt;rd&lt;/sup&gt; year :</p>
<ul>
  <li><a>Engineering</a> and system integration</li>
  <li><a>Software</a> systems and <a>networks</a></li>
  <li>Services and business engineering</li>
  <li><a>Information processing systems</a>.</li>
</ul>
<p> (Mast&#x00e8;res sp&#x00e9;cialis&#x00e9;s, mast&#x00e8;res de recherche, PhDs...)</p>
