<p><strong>Jinan University</strong> (JNU; ) is a leading research and comprehensive university based in <a>Guangzhou</a>, <a>Guangdong</a> province, China.
It is one of the oldest universities established on mainland China tracing back to the Qing Empire.
It is a university administered by the Overseas Chinese Affairs Office of the State Council and the <a>Chinese Ministry of Education</a>.</p>
<h2>History</h2>
<p>In 1906, a Manchu Minister named Duan Fang(&#x7aef;&#x65b9;,1861-1911) submitted a memorial to Emperor Guangxu(&#x5149;&#x7dd2;&#x5e1d;, August 14, 1871&ndash;November 14, 1908), proposing that an oversea Chinese school be set up for the purpose of spreading far and wide the enlightening influence of education and strengthening the emotional ties with compatriots overseas.
Emperor Guangxu approved of his proposal and thus Duan Fang was honored as the founder of Jinan university.. On  March 23rd 1907.
Jinan Academy founded in Nanjing, which serving as a curtain-raiser for the later development of overseas Chinese education.
Henceforth became the first of its kinds in Chinese history.
In 1927, Jinan Academy was renamed Jinan National University, located in Shanghai.
After the found of the <a>People's Republic of China</a> (PRC) in 1949, Jinan University was closed and its schools was merged by the other university such as Fudan University.
In 1958, Jinan University reestablished in <a>Guangzhou</a>.</p>
<p>Though going through ups and downs with the history of the nation since its founding with the name <strong>Chi Nan School</strong> in 1906 at the end of the <a>Qing Dynasty</a>, Jinan University has never lost its way in the pursuit of truth and knowledge.
Under the guidance of the school motto &#x201c;loyalty, sincerity, integrity and respect&#x201d;(&#x5fe0;&#x4fe1;&#x7b03;&#x656c;), in line with the aim of &#x201c;modernization, internationalization and comprehensiveness&#x201d;, Jinan University has been developing quickly in recent years.</p>
<h3>University Name</h3>
<p>The name &quot;Ji-Nan&quot; (also Chi-Nan &quot;reaching southward&quot;) comes from 'The Classic of History' (Chinese: &#x66f8;&#x7d93;/&#x4e66;&#x7ecf;; Pinyin: Sh&#x016b;j&#x012b;ng; ) which is a compilation of documentary records related to events in ancient history of China.
The articles reads, &quot;His speech and teachings spread from north to south, until they reached the four corners of the world.&quot;
Thus the name &quot;Jinan&quot; conveys the school's mission: to spread Chinese culture and learning from North to South, from China to the rest of the world.</p>

<h2>Administration</h2>
<p>Jinan university consist of 20 colleges and 89 research institutes.
The university offers 67 undergraduate degrees, 133 master degree programs, 58 Ph.D degree granting programs, 45 Ph.D programs and 6 post-doctorate research stations.
The university also has 2 national key disciplines and 1 national engineering center.
There are 32,408 students in the university, including 22,174 full-time students, 6,890 graduates and 16,506 undergraduates.
The number of overseas students,including foreign students, overseas Chinese students, students from Hong Kong, Macao and Taiwan, comprise up to 12,108.
</p>
<p>The current president of the university is Dr. Hu Jun.</p>
