<p>The <strong>University of Applied Sciences Giessen-Friedberg</strong> (German: <em>Fachhochschule Gie&#x00df;en-Friedberg</em> or FH Giessen-Friedberg) is a <a>German</a> <a>college</a> for technical studies with <a>campus</a>es in <a>Friedberg</a>, <a>Giessen</a>, and <a>Wetzlar</a>.
Its one of the largest university of applied science in Germany and the largest in the state of <a>Hesse</a>.</p>
