<p><strong>Spelman College</strong> is a four-year <a>liberal arts</a> <a>women's college</a> located in <a>Atlanta, Georgia</a>, United States.
The college is part of the <a>Atlanta University Center</a> academic consortium in Atlanta.

Tapley declared: &quot;Any course of study which fails to cultivate a taste and fitness for practical and efficient work in some part of the field of the world's needs is unpopular at Spelman and finds no place in our curriculum.&quot;
The 2009 U.S. News and World Report also ranked Spelman first among Historically Black Colleges and/or Universities.
</p>
<p>Spelman is accredited by the Commission on Colleges of the <a>Southern Association of Colleges and Schools</a> (SACS).
Spelman is a member of the Coalition of Women's Colleges, National Association of Schools of Music (NASM), National Council for the Accreditation of Teacher Education (NCATE), Southern Association of Colleges and Schools, The College Fund/UNCF, National Association for College Admissions Counseling, and State of Georgia Professional Standards Commission (PSC)&gt;</p>
<p>Spelman offer a Bachelor of Arts degree in the following majors: Art, Child Development, Comparative Women's Studies, Drama &amp; Dance, Economics, English, Foreign Languages (French and Spanish), History, Human Services, Independent Major, International Studies, Mathematics, Music, Philosophy, Political Science, Psychology, Religious Studies, Sociology, and Sociology and Anthropology.</p>
<p>Spelman offer a Bachelor of Science degree in the following majors: Biochemistry, Biology, Chemistry, Computer and Information Sciences, Dual Degree Engineering, Environmental Science, Mathematics, and Physics.</p>
<p>Spelman has a four-year graduation rate of 61%, a five-year graduation rate of 73% and a six-year graduation rate of 74%.</p>
<h3>Honor societies</h3>
<p>Registered honor societies include <a>Alpha Epsilon Delta</a>, <a>Alpha Lambda Delta</a>, <a>Alpha Sigma Lambda</a>, Beta Kappa Chi, <a>Golden Key International Honour Society</a>, <a>Kappa Delta Epsilon</a>, Mortar Board Senior Honor Society, <a>National Society of Collegiate Scholars</a>, <a>Phi Beta Kappa</a>, <a>Pi Sigma Alpha</a>, <a>Psi Chi</a>, <a>Sigma Tau Delta</a>, and the <a>Upsilon Pi Epsilon</a>.&lt;ref
name=&quot;USNews2&quot;/&gt;</p>
<h3>Student publications and media</h3>
<p>Spelman offers a literary magazine (Aunt Chloe: A Journal of Candor), a student newspaper (<em>Spelman Spotlight</em>) and student government association newsletter (<em>Jaguar Print</em>).&lt;ref
name=&quot;USNews2&quot;/&gt;   The yearbook is called <em>Reflections</em>.</p>
<h3>Religious organizations</h3>
<p>Religious organizations currently registered on campus include Baha'i Club, Al-Nissa, Alabaster Box, Atlanta Adventist Collegiate Society, <a>Campus Crusade for Christ</a>, Crossfire International Campus Ministry, Happiness In Praise for His Overflowing Presence, Inter Varsity Christian Fellowship, Movements of Praise Dance Team, The Newman Organization, The Outlet, and The Pre-Theology Society Minority.&lt;ref
name=&quot;USNews2&quot;/&gt;</p>
<h3>International student and social organizations</h3>
<p><a>NAACP</a> and Sister Steps are registered campus organizations.&lt;ref
name=&quot;USNews2&quot;/&gt;</p>
<h3>Athletics</h3>
<p>The sports teams, including <a>basketball</a>, <a>golf</a>, <a>cross-country</a>, <a>soccer</a>, <a>tennis</a>, <a>softball</a>, and <a>volleyball</a> compete in [[NCAA Division III] athletics.
Spelman's mascot is the Jaguar.</p>
