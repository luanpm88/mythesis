<p><strong>Yang-En University</strong> (&#x4ef0;&#x6069;&#x5927;&#x5b66;) is a private university located in the village of <a>Majia</a> outside <a>Quanzhou</a>, <a>Fujian</a>, <a>China</a>.</p>
<p>It was founded in 1987 by Wu Qingxing, an overseas Chinese from <a>Burma</a> whose parents came from Majia.
It is China's first private university.
Wu Qingxing died in 2005 and after that his family members are still involved.</p>
<p>It teaches mainly business subjects plus <a>computer science</a>.
English is emphasized and there are thirteen foreign teachers up to 2010.</p>
<p>Yang-En University is locally famous of issuing strange orders to its employees and students.
It has a bad reputation of breaking contracts.</p>
<p>Comparing with the other private universities,the payment to its employees and acommadation for its students are fairly poor.</p>
