<p> <strong>Samuel Andrew</strong> (January 29, 1656 &ndash; January 24, 1738) was an American <a>Congregational</a> clergyman and educator.
He was born in <a>Cambridge, Massachusetts</a>.
He served as the <a>rector</a> of <a>Yale University</a> between 1707 and 1719.</p>
