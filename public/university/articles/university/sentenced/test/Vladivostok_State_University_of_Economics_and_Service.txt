<p> <a>thumb|220px|Audimax with Videoconferencing</a> <strong>Vladivostok State University of Economics and Service</strong> (), or VSUE (), is a <a>university</a> located in <a>Vladivostok</a>, <a>Russia</a>.</p>
<p>The university was founded in 1967 as <strong>Far Eastern Technological Institute</strong> (), or FETI ().
The university received full accreditation in 1996.</p>
<p>Today, the university offers a large variety of degrees, which include business, management, computer sciences, psychology, sociology, fashion, and others.</p>
