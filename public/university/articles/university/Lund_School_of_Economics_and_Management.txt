<p><a>thumb|Main building</a> The <strong>Lund School of Economics and Management</strong> (LUSEM) or <em>Ekonomih&#x00f6;gskolan i Lund</em> is a <a>business school</a> at <a>Lund University</a> in <a>Lund</a>, <a>Sweden</a>. It has been certified by EQUIS and is a member of EFMD.</p>
<p>It was founded and financed by <a>Holger Crafoord</a>.</p>
<h2>Education</h2>
<p>The Lund School of Economics and Management offers the following Master of Science programs:</p>
<ul>
  <li>Accounting and Auditing</li>
  <li>Accounting and Management Control</li>
  <li>Corporate and Financial Management</li>
  <li>Economic Demography</li>
  <li>Economic Growth, Innovation and Spatial Dynamics</li>
  <li>Economic History</li>
  <li>Economic Research Methods</li>
  <li>Economics</li>
  <li>Entrepreneurship</li>
  <li>Finance</li>
  <li>Information Systems</li>
  <li>International Economics with a focus on China</li>
  <li>International Marketing and Brand Management</li>
  <li>Management Research</li>
  <li>Managing People, Knowledge and Change</li>
  <li>Statistics</li>
  <li>Sustainable Business Leadership</li>
  <li>Technology Management (in cooperation with <a>LTH</a>). </li>
</ul>
