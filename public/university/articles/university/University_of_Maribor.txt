<p>The <strong>University of Maribor</strong> () is the second university in <a>Slovenia</a>, established in 1975. It currently has 17 faculties.</p>
<p>The university's roots reach back to 1859, when a theological seminary was established with the encouragement of Maribor bishop and patriot Anton Martin Slom&#x0161;ek. More faculties were established during the late 50s and early 60s of the 20th century; the Faculties of Economics, Business and Technology in 1959, Agronomy and Law in 1960, and Pedagogy in 1961. The University's opening ceremony occurred on  19 September 1975.</p>
<p>The University has entered the top 1% of institutions in the field of Physics and of the nine institutions entering the top 1% in this field, the University of Maribor did so with the highest number of citations, according to an analysis of data from Essential Science Indicators from Thomson Reuters.</p>
<p>The University of Maribor cooperates in European Framework Programmes since 1998 and the number of international research projects is increasing each year.</p>
<p>According to &quot;Ranking Web of World Universities&quot;, University of Maribor is in the top 15 of &#x201c;Top Central and Eastern Europe Universities&quot;. It is currently (2010) holding the 534th (of 12,000) position of the world universities ranking, which makes it in the top 5% universities of the world.</p>
<h2>Organization</h2>
<p>These are the 17 faculties in which the university is divided into:</p>
<ul>
  <li>Faculty of <a>Agriculture</a></li>
  <li>Faculty of <a>Chemistry</a> and <a>Chemical Engineering</a></li>
  <li>Faculty of <a>Civil Engineering</a></li>
  <li>Faculty of <a>Criminal Justice</a> (in <a>Ljubljana</a>)</li>
  <li>Faculty of <a>Electrical Engineering</a> and <a>Computer Science</a></li>
  <li>Faculty of <a>Economics</a> and <a>Business</a></li>
  <li>Faculty of <a>Education</a></li>
  <li>Faculty of <a>Law</a></li>
  <li>Faculty of <a>Mechanical Engineering</a></li>
  <li>Faculty of <a>Medicine</a></li>
  <li>Faculty of <a>Health care</a></li>
  <li>Faculty of <a>Organizational Studies</a> (in Kranj)</li>
  <li>Faculty of <a>Social Sciences</a> (opened 2007)</li>
  <li><a>Theological</a> Faculty (separated from University of Maribor)</li>
  <li>Faculty of <a>Logistics</a> (in <a>Kr&#x0161;ko</a> and <a>Celje</a> )</li>
  <li>Faculty of <a>Natural sciences and Mathematics</a></li>
  <li>Faculty of <a>Organizational Sciences</a></li>
</ul>
<p>The university also comprises several associated facilities, including the Maribor University Library, student housing, Computing Center, Leon &#x0160;tukelj Sporting Center, and Postgraduate and Visiting Faculty Home.</p>
