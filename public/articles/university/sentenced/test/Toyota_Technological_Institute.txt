<p>The  is a university located in <a>Nagoya</a>, <a>Japan</a>.
Founded in 1981 by a large endowment from <a>Toyota</a> Motors Corporation, it originally only accepted students with some industrial work experience.</p>
<p>In 2003 Toyota also opened the <a>Toyota Technological Institute at Chicago</a>, jointly with the <a>University of Chicago</a>.</p>
