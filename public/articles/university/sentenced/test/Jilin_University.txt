<p> <strong>Jilin University</strong> (; often abbreviated JLU or &#x5409;&#x5927;) is a leading national <a>university</a> under the direct jurisdiction of China's <a>Ministry of Education</a>.</p>
<h2>History</h2>
<p>Founded in 1946 as the <a>Northeast College of Administration</a> in <a>Harbin</a>, <a>Heilongjiang</a>, Jilin University merged with many Universities and colleges and changed its name many times.
In May 1948, the Northeast Administration College merged with the <a>Harbin University</a> and was renamed to Northeast Academy of Science.
In November 1948, the Northeast Academy of Science moved to <a>Shenyang</a>, <a>Liaoning</a> and restored the name of Northeast Administration College. in 1950, it was renamed to the Northeast People's University.
After the <a>Korean War</a> broke out, the Northeast People's University evacuated to <a>Changchun</a>, <a>Jilin</a>.
in 1958, it was renamed &quot;Jilin University.&quot;
In 2000, the <a>Jilin University of Technology</a>, the <a>Bethune Medical University</a>, the <a>Changchun University of Science and Technology</a> and the <a>Changchun College of Posts and Communications</a> merged with JLU.
On August 29, 2004, the <a>University of Military Logistics</a> became another campus of JLU.
The <a>Zhuhai</a> campus was established in 2003.</p>
<p>Located in <a>Changchun</a>, the capital city of <a>Jilin</a> Province in <a>Northeast China</a>, the university has eight campuses in five districts which are home to thirty-nine colleges covering eleven academic disciplines, including <a>philosophy</a>, <a>economics</a>, <a>law</a>, <a>literature</a>, <a>education</a>, <a>history</a>, <a>science</a>, <a>engineering</a>, <a>agriculture</a>, <a>medicine</a>, and <a>management</a>.
The University boasts sixteen disciplinary areas,five national key laboratories, and eight national bases for the development of basic science.
Other resources include five research bases for humanities and social sciences, seven key laboratories sponsored by the Ministry of Education and eleven by other ministries of Chinese government.</p>
<p>Jilin University is a comprehensive and national key university.
JLU offers a variety of degree programs.
It has now 115 <a>undergraduate programs</a>, 192 <a>Master's degree</a> program, 105 <a>doctoral degree</a> programs, and seventeen <a>post-doctoral</a> programs.
In 2003 the university enrolled 59,000 full time students, including more than 10,000 graduate students.
At present there are 5,700 faculty members, with fourteen members of <a>Chinese Academy of Sciences</a> and <a>Chinese Academy of Engineering</a>, 1270 full professors, and 1652 associate professors.</p>
<h2>Facilities</h2>
<p><a>thumb|400px|</a> There are more than fifty multimedia classrooms and language learning audiovisual classrooms.
Special attention has been paid to the construction of 20 basic science laboratories.</p>
<p>The university library claims a collection of 5.26 million books and is designated as the library of <a>United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization</a> (UNESCO), <a>United Nations Industrial Development Organization</a> (UNIDO) and <a>World Bank</a>.
It also serves as the Comprehensive Information Center of Liberal Arts of the Education Ministry, the only Foreign Scientific Text Book Center in Northeast China, and the National Humanities and Social Science Higher Education Books Import Center.
It is one of the seven centers of China Academic Library and Information System (CALIS) Center in Northeast China.</p>
<p>Several campuses have gymnasiums, stadiums, and arenas for basketball, volleyball, table tennis and artistic gymnastics.
The Nanling Campus Stadium, which has a grass soccer field surrounded by standard synthetic racetracks, has 10,000 seats.
There are also tennis and basketball courts throughout the campus.</p>
<p>Jilin University has established ties with more than 130 universities, colleges, and research institutes in 40 countries/districts.
Over 1,200 foreign students currently attend the university.</p>
