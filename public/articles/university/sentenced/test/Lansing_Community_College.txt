<p> <strong>Lansing Community College</strong> is a two-year public college founded in 1957.
The college's main campus is located on an <a>urban</a>, 42-<a>acre</a> (170,000&nbsp;m&#x00b2;) site in downtown <a>Lansing, Michigan</a> spanning seven city blocks.
A West Campus opened in 2004 in <a>Delta Township</a>, southwest of Lansing, and there is also an East Campus located in the Eyde Plaza in <a>East Lansing</a>.
The school is the third largest community college in Michigan by enrollment, with a 2008 enrollment of 20,394 in 2008.</p>
<h2>Athletics</h2>
<p><a>The LCC Athletic Department</a> fields nine intercollegiate teams.
The Lansing Community College Stars play in the <a>Michigan Community College Athletic Association</a>, and is a member of the <a>National Junior College Athletic Association</a>.</p>
<p>Lansing Community College intercollegiate sports include:</p>
<ul>
  <li>Fall   - Men's and Women's Cross Country, Women's Volleyball</li>
  <li>Winter - Men's and Women's Basketball</li>
  <li>Spring - Men's and Women's Track and Field, Softball, Baseball</li>
</ul>
<p>LCC also has a competitive <a>club hockey team</a> that participates in the <a>American Collegiate Hockey Association</a> and the <a>Michigan Collegiate Hockey Conference</a>.</p>
<p>The LCC Athletic Director is Bill Turowski.</p>
<h2>The Library</h2>
<p>As your &quot;Link to Learning&quot; here at LCC, the <a>Library</a> mission is to engage students in learning, link people to resources, and give exceptional service to all.</p>
<h2>The Lookout</h2>
<p>The independent student newspaper for Lansing Community College, <a>The Lookout</a>, is a 16-page, bi-weekly publication that features Campus News, Arts and Entertainment, Sports and a special feature section called &quot;The View&quot;. the Lookout is available at several news stands on campus.</p>

<h2>Extension and Community Education</h2>
<p>The mission of the Office of Extension and Community Education at Lansing Community College is to provide learning and teaching opportunities in off-campus locations with the same standards of excellence as on campus.
For a full range of classes at off-campus sites consult the ECE web page on the LCC web site - <a>lcc.edu/ece</a></p>
