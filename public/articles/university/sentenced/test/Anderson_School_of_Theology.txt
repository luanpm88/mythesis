<p> <strong>Anderson University School of Theology</strong> is the graduate theological school affiliated with the <a>Church of God (Anderson)</a>.
It is a part of  <a>Anderson University</a> and receives students from <a>Azusa Pacific University</a>, <a>Bay Ridge Christian College</a>, <a>Gardner College</a>, <a>Mid-America Christian University</a>, <a>Warner Pacific College</a>, <a>Warner University</a>, and other schools.
</p>
<h2>Academics and Demographics</h2>
<p>It is mainly associated with the <a>Wesleyan</a>-<a>Holiness</a> tradition.
It is an accredited institution which grants the following degrees:</p>
<ul>
  <li>Doctor of Ministry (D.
Min.)</li>
  <li>Master of Divinity (M.Div.)</li>
<li>Master of Theological Studies (M.T.S.)</li>
  <li>Master of Arts in Intercultural Service (M.A.I.S.)</li>
  <li>an online Master of Arts in Christian Ministries (M.A.C.M.).</li>
</ul>
<h2>History</h2>
<p>The school was established in 1950 for the training of ministers in the Church of God (Anderson).
