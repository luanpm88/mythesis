<p>The <strong>Universit&#x00e9; du Qu&#x00e9;bec</strong> is a system of ten provincially-run public <a>universities</a> in <a>Quebec</a>, <a>Canada</a>.
Its headquarters are in <a>Quebec City</a>.
The university has more than 87,000 students, making it the largest university in Canada.
It offers more than 300 programs.
The government of Quebec founded the Universit&#x00e9; du Qu&#x00e9;bec, a network of universities in several Quebec cities.
In a similar fashion to other Canadian provinces, all universities in Quebec have since become public.</p>
<h2>History</h2>
<p>[[Image:ETS-soir.jpg|140px|right|thumb|[[&#x00c9;cole de technologie sup&#x00e9;rieure]]]] <a>140px|right|thumb|Universit&#x00e9; du Qu&#x00e9;bec a Chicoutimi</a> It was created in 1968 by an act of Quebec's National Assembly.
The component institutions are:</p>
<ul>
  <li>the <a>&#x00c9;cole de technologie sup&#x00e9;rieure</a> (ETS), in <a>Montreal</a>;</li>
  <li>the <a>&#x00c9;cole nationale d'administration publique</a> (ENAP), based in <a>Quebec City</a>, with campuses in Montreal, Gatineau, Trois-Rivi&#x00e8;res, and Saguenay;</li>
  <li>the <a>Institut national de la recherche scientifique</a> (INRS), based in <a>Quebec City</a> and a campus in Montreal;</li>
  <li>the <a>Universit&#x00e9; du Qu&#x00e9;bec &#x00e0; Chicoutimi</a> (UQAC), in <a>Saguenay</a>;</li>
  <li>the <a>Universit&#x00e9; du Qu&#x00e9;bec &#x00e0; Montr&#x00e9;al</a> (UQAM), in <a>Montreal</a>;</li>
  <li>the <a>Universit&#x00e9; du Qu&#x00e9;bec &#x00e0; Rimouski</a> (UQAR), in <a>Rimouski</a>;</li>
  <li>the <a>Universit&#x00e9; du Qu&#x00e9;bec &#x00e0; Trois-Rivi&#x00e8;res</a> (UQTR), in <a>Trois-Rivi&#x00e8;res</a>;</li>
  <li>the <a>Universit&#x00e9; du Qu&#x00e9;bec en Abitibi-T&#x00e9;miscamingue</a> (UQAT), with campuses in <a>Val-d'Or</a>, <a>Rouyn-Noranda</a>, and other towns;</li>
  <li>the <a>Universit&#x00e9; du Qu&#x00e9;bec en Outaouais</a> (UQO), in <a>Gatineau</a>.</li>
</ul>
<p><a>T&#x00e9;l&#x00e9;-Universit&#x00e9;</a> (T&#x00e9;luq) was originally an independent component of the network, based in <a>Quebec City</a>, with offices in <a>Montreal</a>, but was made an affiliate of <a>UQAM</a> in 2005.</p>
