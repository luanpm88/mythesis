<p>The <strong>Chalmers Lindholmen University College</strong> or <em>Chalmers Lindholmen</em> was a <a>university college</a>, and an affiliate of <a>Chalmers University of Technology</a>, located in <a>Gothenburg</a>, <a>Sweden</a>.
The campus is located at <a>Lindholmen</a> on the island <a>Hisingen</a>.
As of 2005 Chalmers Lindholmen no longer exists as a separate organisation.
Campus Lindholmen is now one of Chalmers' two campuses.</p>
<h2>Schools</h2>
<p>The organization is divided into schools.</p>
<ul>
  <li>School of Engineering</li>
  <li>School of Maritime Studies</li>
  <li>School of Continuing and Professional Learning</li>
  <li>Centre of Research and Development </li>
</ul>
<h2>Students</h2>
<p>Around 2,700 undergraduate students are attending Chalmers Lindholmen University College.</p>
