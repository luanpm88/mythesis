<p><strong>Corning Community College</strong> in the <a>U.S. state</a> of <a>New York</a> was initiated in 1957 and moved to its Spencer Hill campus in 1963. This two-year college serves three counties: <a>Steuben</a>, <a>Chemung</a>, and <a>Schuyler</a>. It is one of the <a>community colleges</a> in the <a>SUNY</a> system.</p>
<h2>Campus and programs</h2>
<p>The college offers Associate of Sciences, <a>Associate of Arts</a>, Applied Associate of Science, Associate of Occupation Studies and a number of certificates as well.</p>
<p>The college also operates the <a>Eileen M. Collins Observatory</a>.</p>
