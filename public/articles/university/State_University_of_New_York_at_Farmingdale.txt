<p>The <strong>Farmingdale State College</strong>, also known as <strong>SUNY Farmingdale</strong>, and also called <strong>Farmingdale State College</strong>, is the former <strong>Long Island</strong> <strong>Agricultural and Technical Institute or LIATI.</strong> This college is located on <a>Long Island</a> in <a>East Farmingdale, New York</a>, with a small section in <a>Old Bethpage</a>. The college dates from 1912 as a school of applied agriculture. Currently the college offers bachelor degrees and associate degrees across various fields. </p>
