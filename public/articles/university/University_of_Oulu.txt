<p>The <strong>University of Oulu</strong> () is one of the largest universities in <a>Finland</a>, located in the city of <a>Oulu</a>. It was founded on July 8, 1958. The university has around 17,000 students and 3,000 staff. It is ranked in the <a>Academic Ranking of World Universities</a> (compiled by <a>Shanghai Jiao Tong University</a>) as the second best university in Finland along with the University of Turku and 303&#x2013;401 worldwide.</p>
<h2>History</h2>
<ul>
  <li>1919 Oulu College Association</li>
  <li>1958 Oulu University Act</li>
  <li>1959 Activities begin. Faculty of Philosophy, Faculty of Technology and Oulu Teaching School</li>
  <li>1960 Faculty of Medicine</li>
  <li>1965 Teaching begins in Humanities</li>
  <li>1972 Faculty of Humanities</li>
  <li>1974 Faculty of Education</li>
  <li>1985 Professorship in Telecommunications Technology established at the Faculty of Technology</li>
  <li>1994 Biotechnology, Information Technology and Northern Issues defined as focus areas</li>
  <li>2000 Founding of the Faculty of Economics and Business Administration</li>
  <li>2010 New University Act, new focus areas</li>
</ul>
<h2>Education</h2>
<h3>Campuses</h3>
<p>There are four campus areas:</p>
<ul>
  <li>The main campus is located in Linnanmaa, about  north of <a>Oulu</a> city centre. It includes 5 faculties,  <a>Infotech Oulu</a>, <a>Thule Institute</a>, <a>Giellagas Institute</a>, <a>Martti Ahtisaari Institute</a> , <a>CWC</a>, <a>CIE</a>, <a>Centre for Advanced Steel Research (CASR)</a> and <a>Micro and Nanotechnology Centre</a>;</li>
  <li>Faculty of Medicine has its own campus area and it is fully integrated in the city's hospital  from the city center, Kontinkangas, Oulu. <a>Biocenter Oulu</a>, Clinical Research Centre (CRC) and <a>Welltech Oulu</a> are also located there;</li>
  <li>The third campus area is located in <a>Kajaani</a>, about  southeast of Oulu.</li>
  <li><a>Department of Architecture</a> which is located in the center of Oulu</li>
</ul>
<p>The University has a number of research stations and shared locations, most notably:</p>

<h3>International Cooperation</h3>
<p>University of Oulu aims to promote mobility among its students. It takes part in a number of international exchange programmes, such as <a>Erasmus Programme</a> (Europe), <a>Nordplus</a> (Nordic Countries), <a>FIRST</a> (Russia), <a>ISEP</a> and International to International ISEP (USA, South America, Asia), <a>UNC-EP</a> (USA), <a>north2north</a> (USA, Canada, Russia, Nordic Countries), <a>North-South-South</a> (Africa). Besides, University of Oulu has about 50 bilateral agreements with partner universities around the world.</p>
<h3>International Master's degree programmes</h3>
<p>The official language of teaching is Finnish. There are also 12 <a>international Master's degree programmes</a> which are offered fully in English:</p>
<ul>
  <li><a>Architectural Design</a></li>
  <li><a>Astronomy and Space Physics</a></li>
  <li><a>Barents Environmental Engineering (BEE)</a></li>
  <li><a>Education and Globalisation (EdGlo)</a></li>
  <li><a>Finance and Economics (FE)</a></li>
  <li><a>Financial and Management Accounting (FMA)</a></li>
  <li><a>Health and Wellbeing in the Circumpolar Area</a></li>
  <li><a>International Business (IB)</a></li>
  <li><a>Protein Science and Biotechnology</a></li>
  <li><a>Software, Systems and Services Development in Global Environment (GS3D)</a></li>
  <li><a>Synchrotron Radiation Based Science and Accelerator Physics</a></li>
  <li><a>Wireless Communications Engineering (WCE)</a></li>

<p>The application procedure to the Master&#x2019;s programmes varies according to the programme. There is only one application period per academic year. The university uses an online application system and the application period is open for two to three months.</p>
<p>Each Master&#x2019;s programme has its own specific admissions requirements. In general, to apply for Master&#x2019;s level studies in the University of Oulu applicants are required to hold a Bachelor&#x2019;s level degree with good or excellent merits from a higher education institution which entitles you to apply for Master&#x2019;s level studies in their home country and have a good command of English.</p>
<h2>Research</h2>
<p>University works in 70 research areas. At the same time, four focus research areas are:</p>
<ul>
  <li>Information Technology (Machine vision and ubiquitous computing technologies, Wireless communications, High-speed electronics and photonics, Biomedical engineering, Information systems and software)</li>
  <li>Biosciences and Health (Cell-extracellular matrix research, Cardiovascular disease research, Enzyme structure and metabolic research, Genes and the environment)</li>
  <li>Cultural Identity and Interaction (Cultural Identity and Interaction; Language, education and interaction)</li>
  <li>Environment, Natural Resources and Materials (Environmental issues, natural resources and energy economy; materials science, properties and behaviour of matter, catalysts and material innovations; modelling and computational science)</li>
</ul>
<p>New initiatives involve advanced steel research, international business, mining and mineral engineering and science-based education.</p>
<p>The University of Oulu is known among <a>hackers</a> as the home of the <a>Internet Relay Chat</a> and for the <a>File Transfer Protocol</a> server x2ftp, named after the basement / blast shelter area in which it was physically located. x2ftp gained its reputation among <a>computer game</a> developers and members of the <a>demoscene</a> for containing a very good archive of information pertaining to low-level graphics programming.</p>

<h2>Student's life</h2>
<h3>Student union</h3>
<p>Student Union of the University of Oulu (; <em>OYY</em>) provides services to and supervises the interests of all undergraduate degree students in the University of Oulu. All Bachelor's and Master's degree students in the University of Oulu belong to the Student Union. By paying the Student Union membership fee students are entitled for a student card which allows them to get student services and discounts. This way, students get inexpensive or free medical and dental care from Finnish Student Health Services, (; <em>YTHS</em>) traveling discounts in trains and buses, student meals reduction etc.</p>
<h3>Student guilds</h3>
<p>There is a number of students organizations or so called student guilds presented within the university. They cover a variety of interests. Guilds arrange various info events, gettogethers, parties, trips, sports events and often have their own room in the university where students can have coffee, use computers, meet people, play video games or watch TV etc. Each student guild also has its own <a>overalls</a>, with their specific colour. Students usually wear overalls for various parties and outside activities to create a certain feeling of togetherness.</p>
<h3>Student housing</h3>
<p>Student Housing Foundation of Northern Finland (; <em>PSOAS</em>) is the leading provider of student accommodation in Oulu. The organization operates on a foundation basis which ensures affordable rents for students. Following its mission, PSOAS offers housing services for people studying in Oulu as cheaply as possible. </p>
<p>During the year 2011 PSOAS intends to build a new international student house right next to its housing office in downtown Oulu. The house will be accommodated both by international degree students and Finnish students. The main purpose of the new building is to support the internationalization of Oulu and give the international students a better access to Finnish culture and local people. The premises will also support these efforts: More space will be dedicated for casual get-togethers and an international caf&#x00e9; of high level will be serving both Finnish and foreign customers.</p>
