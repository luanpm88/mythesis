<p>The <strong>Duale Hochschule Baden W&#x00fc;rttemberg</strong> (DHBW) - Cooperative State University Mosbach - in <a>Germany</a> offers all enrolled applicants a degree program qualifying them for a profession through a tight combination of theory and practice. The aim is to offer students a practical but at the same time scientific-based degree which is an attractive alternative to other tertiary education degrees.</p>
<p>A very specific characteristic of all Cooperative Education Universities consist in tight arrangements with Enterprises in Germany, which allows students to spend several months per year in practical work (as much as half of the academic year). These Enterprises represent all business activities in Germany such as Technology and Banking.</p>
