<p>  <strong>Golden West College</strong> or <strong>GWC</strong>, is a <a>community college</a> providing two-year associate of arts degrees, and lower-division classes transferable to other colleges and universities. It is located in <a>Huntington Beach, California</a>, about 35 miles (58&nbsp;km) south of <a>Los Angeles, California</a>.</p>
<h2>Organization</h2>
<p>The college is one of three in the <a>Coast Community College District</a>, a regional organization providing administrative services and funding for post-secondary education. The district is chartered by the state of California to provide community college services.</p>
<h2>Academic profile</h2>
<p>The mission of GWC is to provide inexpensive education in the trades, licensed trades and skilled professions. as well as remedial and transferable lower-division courses for students who plan to transfer to either a <a>California State University</a> or a campus of the <a>University of California</a>.</p>
