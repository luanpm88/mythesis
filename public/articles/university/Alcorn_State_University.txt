<p> <strong>Alcorn State University</strong>, a <a>historically black university</a>, is a comprehensive land-grant institution. </p>
<h2>Academics</h2>
<p>The university enrolls over 2,500 full-time and 348 part-time undergraduate students and 600 graduate students.  Women outnumber men in a ratio 3:2.</p>
<h3>Organization</h3>
<p>The university is made up of seven schools, offering over 50 different fields of study.</p>
<ul>
  <li>School of Agriculture, Research, Extension and Applied Sciences</li>
  <li>School of Arts and Sciences</li>
  <li>School of Graduate Studies</li>
  <li>School of Business</li>
  <li>School of Education and Psychology</li>
  <li>School of Nursing</li>
  <li>College for Excellence</li>
</ul>
<h2>Campus</h2>
<p>The main campus is located near <a>Lorman, Mississippi</a>, while the Nursing School and the Business School's Master of Business Administration (MBA) program is located in <a>Natchez, Mississippi</a>. There is also a <a>Vicksburg, Mississippi</a> site. </p>
<p>Recent Campus Additions include:</p>
<ul>
  <li>Clinton Bristow Jr. Dining Commons</li>
  <li>Biotechnology Facility</li>
  <li> Bike/Walking Trail</li>
  <li>Ecology and Natural Resources Facility</li>
  <li>Willie &quot;Rat&quot; McGowan Baseball Stadium </li>
  <li>Fine Arts Renovation</li>
  <li>Medgar Wiley Evers Heritage Village (Student Housing)</li>
</ul>
<p>Current and planned construction projects include: </p>
<ul>
  <li>School of Business (Dumas Hall) renovations</li>
</ul>
