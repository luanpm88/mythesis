<p>:  <strong>Samuel Locke</strong> (November 23, 1731 &ndash; January 15, 1778) was a <a>U.S.</a> <a>Congregational</a> clergyman and educator.  After serving as pastor in <a>Sherborn, Massachusetts</a>, he was appointed <a>president</a> of <a>Harvard University</a>.  He held that post from 1770 to 1773 when he resigned.  He then returned to Sherborn where he died of <a>apoplexy</a>.</p>
