<p> <strong>Lule&#x00e5; University of Technology</strong> or <em>Lule&#x00e5; tekniska universitet</em> of <a>Sweden</a> is Scandinavia's northernmost university of technology. It has four <a>campus</a>es, located in <a>Lule&#x00e5;</a> (the main campus), <a>Kiruna</a> (space science), <a>Skellefte&#x00e5;</a> (wood technology and computer game engineering) and <a>Pite&#x00e5;</a> (Dep of music and media).</p>
<h2>History</h2>
<p>The university was founded on June 1, 1971 at Pors&#x00f6;n in Lule&#x00e5; as <em>H&#x00f6;gskoleenheten i Lule&#x00e5;</em>. The name was later changed to <em><a>H&#x00f6;gskola</a>n i Lule&#x00e5;</em> (<em>Lule&#x00e5; University College</em>). In 1997, it was granted university status by the Swedish government, and is since known as <em>Lule&#x00e5; University of Technology</em>.</p>
<p>In 1977 it was merged with the older Teacher's Training College of Lule&#x00e5; and a year later <a>Pite&#x00e5; School of Music</a> (Musikh&#x00f6;gskolan i Pite&#x00e5;) was created.</p>
<h2>Students' unions</h2>
<p><a>thumb|Lule&#x00e5; University of Technology</a> As of <a>2005</a>, there are three <a>students' union</a>s at the university, <em>Teknologk&#x00e5;ren vid Lule&#x00e5; tekniska univeristet</em> (the <em>Student Association of Engineering at Lule&#x00e5; University of Technology</em>) for students studying towards an engineering or other degree within the field of technology, <em>Pite&#x00e5; Studentk&#x00e5;r</em> (the <em>Students' Union in Pite&#x00e5;</em>) and <em>Lule&#x00e5; studentk&#x00e5;r</em> (<em>Lule&#x00e5; Students' Union</em>) for most other students.</p>
<p>The unions are divided into sections, each responsible for a somewhat arbitrary number of academic programmes. They are run by a board of eight to ten students for no profit.</p>
<p>Lule&#x00e5; Students Union has the following sections:</p>
<ul>
  <li>SNS or <em>Statsvetar- och Nationalekonomsektionen</em> for <a>political science</a> and <a>economics</a></li>
  <li><em>Pedagogsektionen</em> for <a>teacher education</a></li>
  <li>SySek or <em>Systemvetarsektionen</em> for <a>systems science</a></li>
  <li>EkSek or <em>Ekonomsektionen</em> for <a>business administration</a></li>
  <li>PuSS or <em>Psykologi- und Sociologisektionen</em> for <a>psychiatry</a> and <a>sociology</a></li>
  <li>JF or <em>Juridiska F&#x00f6;reningen</em> for <a>jurisprudence</a></li>
  <li>BofS or <em>Bodens F&#x00f6;renade Studenter</em> for students in <a>Boden</a></li>
  <li>LEKIS or <em>L&#x00e4;rare och Ekonomer i Skellefte&#x00e5;</em> for teaching and business students in <a>Skellefte&#x00e5;</a></li>
</ul>
<p>The Student Association of Engineering has the following sections:</p>
<ul>
  <li><em><a>Datasektionen</a></em>[<a>http://www.datasektionen.com/</a>] for <a>computer science</a>, <a>space engineering</a>, <a>engineering physics</a> and some other areas</li>
  <li><em>Geosektionen</em>[<a>http://geo.studorg.ltu.se/</a>] for <a>civil engineering</a>, <a>geotechnical engineering</a>, <a>chemical engineering</a>, <a>environmental engineering</a> and many other programmes</li>
  <li><em>Industriell managementsektionen [<a>http://www.i-sektionen.se/</a>]</em> for Industrial Management and Engineering</li>
  <li><em>Kirunasektionen</em> for students in <a>Kiruna</a></li>
  <li><em>Maskinteknologsektionen</em>[<a>http://msekt.studorg.luth.se/</a>] for <a>mechanical engineering</a> and related programmes</li>
  <li><em>Skellefte&#x00e5; teknologsektion</em> for engineering students in <a>Skellefte&#x00e5;</a></li>
  <li><em>Doktoranderna</em> for PhD students</li>
</ul>
