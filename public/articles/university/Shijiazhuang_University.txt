<p><strong>Shijiazhuang College</strong> (&#x77f3;&#x5bb6;&#x5e84;&#x5b66;&#x9662;) is a university located in <a>Shijiazhuang</a>, <a>Hebei</a>, <a>China</a> under the provincial government. </p>
<h2>Location</h2>
<p>There are two campuses; North Campus and South Campus with 82 hectares of land and 350,000 square meters of floor space. </p>
