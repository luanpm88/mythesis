<p><strong>Niagara County Community College</strong> is located in <a>Sanborn, New York</a>, <a>USA</a> northeast of the City of <a>Niagara Falls</a>. NCCC offers <a>associate's degree</a>s in many programs. Niagara County Community College was founded in 1962 and is sponsored by <a>Niagara County</a> and <a>SUNY</a>. The new campus opened in 1973. Dual admissions programs facilitate transfer to four-year colleges upon completion of the two-year degree programs. </p>

<h2>Campus</h2>
<p>The campus is located on the corner of <a>New York State Route 31</a> and <a>New York State Route 429</a>. It consists of  of semi-wooded land and eight interconnected buildings described as &quot;architecturally striking.&quot;</p>
<ul>
  <li>The Ernest Notar Administration Building contains the colleges administrative offices. It is named for Dr. Ernest Notar, the first president of the college from 1962-1975.</li>
  <li>The Business Education Building is the location of the Business Division.</li>
  <li>The Science/Technology Building is the home of the Life Sciences, Nursing, and Mathematics/Computer Science/Physics and Engineering/Technology/Chemistry Divisions.</li>
  <li>The Henrietta G. Lewis Library is the most architecturally striking building and has a collection of 95,391 books. &lt;!-- The 2nd and 3rd floor bathrooms seem to be the least used. --&gt;</li>
  <li>The Humanities/Social Sciences Building houses the English, literature, linguistics, languages, philosophy, history, government, psychology, sociology, anthropology, and economics departments.</li>
  <li>The Fine Arts Building contains the Fine Arts Division and the college's auditorium.</li>
  <li>The Student Center holds culinary arts classes the college bookstore, student lounges, a game room, a bowling alley.</li>
  <li>The Health Education Center includes a gym, which seats 2,000, fitness center, and a swimming pool, which seats 200.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Athletics </h2>
<p>The college has had a rich history in athletics in the past and has been known for their acceleration in <a>wrestling</a> and <a>baseball</a>  They were formerly the Trailblazers and became the <a>Thunderwolves</a> in 2010 and hired a former athlete and head coach Bob McKeown.</p>
<h2>Future</h2>
<p>NCCC plans on creating a hospitality school, a culinary arts school, a restaurant and a bookstore in the heart of Downtown <a>Niagara Falls, New York</a> in the former <a>Rainbow Centre Factory Outlet</a>.</p>
